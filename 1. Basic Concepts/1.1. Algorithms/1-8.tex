\( (m, n) \to a^mb^n \).

The idea is to \( (m, n) \to (|m - n|, \min(m, n)) \).

\subsubsection*{1.1.}

To find \( | m - n | \) we can remove middle \( ab \) until they all gone. We will be left with

\item \( a^{m-n} \), if \( m > n \).
\item \( \varepsilon \), empty word, if \( m = n \).
\item \( b^{n-m} \), if \( m < n \).

\subsubsection*{1.2.}

If we then replace all \( b \) with \( a \), we will get \( a^{|m-n|} \).

However, with this approach, we lost \( \min(m, n) \), which is exactly the amount of removals in 1.1.

So we need another approach.

\subsubsection*{2.1}

We need to replace \( ab \) with some additional character \( c \).

\( a^mb^n \to a^{m-1}cb^{n-1} \)

\subsubsection*{2.2}

Then we need to move \( c \) to the end. Via repeating replacement \( cb \to bc \).

\( a^{m-1}cb^{n-1} \to a^{m-1}bcb^{n-2} \to \cdots \to a^{m-1}b^{n-1}c \)

Then we go to 2 and repeat. We will get

\item \( a^{m-n}c^n \), if \( m > n \)
\item \( c^n \), if \( m = n \)
\item \( b^{n-m}c^m \), if \( m < n \)

\subsubsection*{2.3.}

Then replace all \( b \to a \).

\subsubsection*{2.4.}

Then replace all \( c \to b \).

And we get \( a^{|m-n|}b^{\min(m,n)} \).

Go to \textbf{2.1}.

\subsubsection*{2.5.}

When we get \( a^m \), \( m \) - is a desired gcd of original \( m \) and \( n \).

\subsubsection*{Final solution}

\( A := \{ a, b, c \} \)

\( N := 5 \)

\( \theta_0 := b \)

\( \phi_0 := b \)

\( a_0 := 5, (a^m, 0) \to (a^m, 5) \)

\( b_0 := 1, (a^mb^n, 0) \to (a^mb^n, 1) \)

\( \theta_1 := ab \)

\( \phi_1 := c \)

\( a_1 := 3, (a^mc^p, 1) \to (a^mc^p, 3), (b^nc^p, 1) \to (b^nc^p, 3) \)

\( b_1 := 2, (a^mb^nc^p, 1) \to (a^{m-1}cb^{n-1}c^p, 2) \)

\( \theta_2 = cb \)

\( \phi_2 = bc \)

\( a_2 = 1, (a^mb^nc^p, 2) \to (a^mb^nc^p, 1) \)

\( b_2 = 2, (a^mb^ncb^pc^q, 2) \to (a^mb^{n+1}cb^{p-1}c^q, 2) \)

\( \theta_3 = b \)

\( \phi_3 = a \)

\( a_3 = 4, (a^mc^p, 3) \to (a^mc^p, 4) \)

\( b_3 = 3, (a^mb^nc^p, 3) \to (a^{m + 1}b^{n-1}c^p, 3) \)

\( \theta_4 = c \)

\( \phi_4 = b \)

\( a_4 = 0, (a^mb^n, 4) \to (a^mb^n, 0) \)

\( b_4 = 4, (a^mb^nc^p, 4) \to (a^mb^{n+1}c^{p-1}, 4) \)

\( \theta_5 = \varepsilon \)

\( \phi_i = \varepsilon \)
