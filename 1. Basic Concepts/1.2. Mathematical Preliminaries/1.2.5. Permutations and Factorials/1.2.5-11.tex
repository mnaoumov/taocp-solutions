\( \mu(n,2) = \sum_{k > 0}{\lfloor{\frac{n}{2^{k}}}\rfloor} \)

\( n = \sum_{i=1}^{r} 2^{e_{i}} \)

\( \mu(n,2) = \sum_{k > 0}{\lfloor{\sum_{i=1}^{r} 2^{e_{i}-k}}\rfloor} = \sum_{k > 0}{\lfloor{\overbrace{\sum_{\substack{1 \leq i \leq r\\ e_{i} \geq k}} 2^{e_{i}-k}}^{A} + \overbrace{\sum_{\substack{1 \leq i \leq r\\ e_{i} < k}} 2^{e_{i}-k}}^{B} }\rfloor} \)

\( A \in \mathbb{Z} \)

\( 0 \leq B \leq \sum_{j=0}^{k-1} 2^{j-k} = 1 - 2^{-k} < 1 \)

\( \mu(n,2) = \sum_{k > 0}{\lfloor{A+B}\rfloor} = \sum_{k > 0} A = \sum_{k>0} \sum_{\substack{1 \leq i \leq r\\ e_{i} \geq k}} 2^{e_{i}-k} = \)

\( = \sum_{i=1}^{r} 2^{e_{i}} \sum_{\substack{k>0, k \leq e_{i}}}{2^{-k}} = \sum_{i=1}^r{2^{e_{i}}(1-2^{-e_{i}})} = \sum_{i=1}^{r}(2^{e_{i}}-1) = n - r \)
