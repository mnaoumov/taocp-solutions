Introduce the following notation: \( a = \langle{b} \rangle_{p} \overset{\mathrm{def}}{\iff} a \mod p = b \mod p \)

\( n = \sum_{i \geq 0}{a_{i} p^{i}} \)

\( a_{i} = 0, i > k \)

\( n_{j} := \lfloor{\frac{n}{p^{j}}}\rfloor = \sum_{i \geq j}{a_{i} p^{i-j}} \)

\( n = n_{0} = pn_{1} + a_{0} \)

\( n_{k} = a_{k} \)

\( n! = \)

\( = \overbrace{1 \cdot 2  \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (p - 1)}^{A} \cdot \overbrace{p}^{B} \cdot \)

\( \cdot \overbrace{(p+1)(p+2) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (p+(p-1))}^{A} \cdot \overbrace{2p}^{B} \cdot \)

\( \cdot \overbrace{(2p+1)(2p+2) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (2p+(p-1))}^{A} \cdot \overbrace{3p}^{B} \cdot \)

\( \cdots \)

\( \cdot \overbrace{((n_{1}-1)p+1)((n_{1}-1)p+2) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot ((n_{1}-1)p+(p-1))}^{A} \cdot \overbrace{n_{1}p}^{B} \cdot \)

\( \cdot \overbrace{(n_{1}p+1)(n_{1}p+2) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (n_{1}p+a_{0})}^{C} \)

\( \forall j, 0 \leq j \leq n_{1} - 1: (jp+1)(jp+2) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (jp+p-1) = \langle{1 \cdot 2 \cdots (p-1)}\rangle_{p} = \langle{(p-1)!}\rangle_{p} = \langle{-1}\rangle_{p} \)

\( \implies A = \langle{(-1)}^{n_{1}}\rangle_{p} \)

\( B = n_{1}!p^{n_{1}} \)

\( C = \langle{a_{0}!}\rangle_{p} \)

\( n! = n_{0}! = ABC = \langle{{(-1)}^{n_{1}}}\rangle_{p} n_{1}!p^{n_{1}}\langle{a_{0}!}\rangle_{p} \)

Similarly

\( n_{1}! = \langle{{(-1)}^{n_{2}}}\rangle_{p} n_{2}!p^{n_{2}}\langle{a_{1}!}\rangle_{p} \)

\( \vdots \)

\( n_{k-1}! = \langle{{(-1)}^{n_{k}}}\rangle_{p} n_{k}!p^{n_{k}}\langle{a_{k-1}!}\rangle_{p} \)

\( n_{k}! = a_{k}! \)

\( n! = (\langle{{(-1)}^{n_{1}}}\rangle_{p} p^{n_{1}}\langle{a_{0}!}\rangle_{p}) \cdot (\langle{{(-1)}^{n_{2}}}\rangle_{p} p^{n_{2}}\langle{a_{1}!}\rangle_{p}) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot \cdot (\langle{{(-1)}^{n_{k}}}\rangle_{p} p^{n_{k}}\langle{a_{k-1}!}\rangle_{p}) \cdot a_{k}! = \)

\( = \langle{{(-1)}^{n_{1}+n_{2}+ \cdots + n_{k}}}\rangle_{p} p^{n_{1}+n_{2}+\cdots +n_{k}}\langle{a_{0}!a_{1}! \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot a_{k}!}\rangle_{p} \)

\( n_{1}+n_{2}+\cdots+n_{k} = \mu(n,p) \)

\( \frac{n!}{p^{\mu(n,p)}} = \langle{{(-1)}^{\mu(n,p)}a_{0}!a_{1}! \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot a_{k}!}\rangle_{p} \)

\( \frac{n!}{p^{\mu(n,p)}} \equiv {(-1)}^{\mu(n,p)}a_{0}!a_{1}! \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot a_{k}! \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ p) \)
