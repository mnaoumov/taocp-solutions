\( \frac{(n+x)!}{n!} \approx n^{x} \)

\( \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{(n+x)!}{n! \cdot n^{x}} = 1 \)

For \( x = m \in \mathbb{N}_{0} \)

\( \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{(n+m)!}{n! \cdot n^{m}} = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{(n+1)(n+2)\cdot\quad\cdots\quad\cdot(n+m) \cdot m!}{n^{m}} = \lim_{n \to \infty} (1+\frac{1}{n})(1+\frac{2}{n})\cdots(1+\frac{m}{n}) = 1 \)

\( (n+x)! = (n+x)(n+x-1)! = (n+x)(n-1+x)(n+x-2)! = \cdots = (n+x)(n-1+x)\cdots(1+x)\cdot x! \)

\( x! = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{n! \cdot n^{x}}{(x+1)(x+2)\cdots (x+n)} \)
