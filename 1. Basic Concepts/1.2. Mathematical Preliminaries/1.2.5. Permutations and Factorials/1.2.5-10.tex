\( \forall x \in \mathbb{R} \setminus \{ 0, -1, -2, \ldots \}: x\Gamma(x) = \lim_{m\to\infty} \frac{m^{x}m!}{(x+1)(x+2) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (x+m)} =   \lim_{m\to\infty} \frac{{(m+1)}^{x}(m+1)!}{(x+1)(x+2) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (x+m+1)} = \)

\( = \lim_{m\to\infty}\frac{{(m+1)}^{x+1}m!}{(x+1)((x+1)+1) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot ((x+1)+m)} = \lim_{m\to\infty}\frac{{(1+\frac{1}{m})}^{x+1}m^{x+1}m!}{(x+1)((x+1)+1) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot ((x+1)+m)} = \)

\( = \lim_{m\to\infty}{{(1+\frac{1}{m})}^{x+1}} \cdot \lim_{m\to\infty}\frac{m^{x+1}m!}{(x+1)((x+1)+1) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot ((x+1)+m)} = 1 \cdot \Gamma(x+1) = \Gamma(x+1) \)
