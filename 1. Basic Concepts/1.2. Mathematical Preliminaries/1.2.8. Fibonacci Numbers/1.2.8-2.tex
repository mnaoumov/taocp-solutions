\( F_{1000} \approx \frac{\phi^{1000}}{\sqrt{5}} \)

\( \ln{F_{1000}} \approx \ln(\frac{\phi^{1000}}{\sqrt{5}}) = 1000 \ln{\phi} - \frac{1}{2} \ln{5} \approx 1000 \cdot 0.4812118250 - \frac{1}{2} \cdot 1.60943 \approx 480.40711 \)

\( \log_{10}{F_{1000}} = \ln_{F_{1000}} \cdot \frac{1}{\ln{10}} \approx 480.40711 \cdot 0.43429 \approx 208.64 \)

\( F_{1000} = 10^{\log_{10}{F_{1000}}} \approx 10^{208} \cdot 10^{0.64} \approx 4.3 \cdot 10^{208} \)
