\( n \) is not prime, \( n > 4 \) \( \implies n = kl, 2 \leq k \leq l, l \geq 3 \)

\( F_{l} \geq 3 \implies F_{l} > 1 \)

\( F_{n} = F_{kl}, F_{l} \mid F_{n}, F_{n} > F_{l} \implies \frac{F_{n}}{F_{l}} > 1 \implies F_{n} \) is not prime

For \( n = 4, k=l=2, l \not \geq 3, F_{n} = 3 \) --- exception.
