\section*{(4)}

\( F_{n + 1}F_{n-1} - {(F_{n})}^{2} = {(-1)}^{n}, n \geq 1 \quad (4) \)

To prove for all \( n \in \mathbb{Z} \), we need to do reverse induction

\( (n \implies n - 1) \)

\( F_{n}F_{n-2} - {(F_{n-1})}^{2} = F_{n}(F_{n} - F_{n-1}) - {(F_{n-1})}^{2} = {(F_{n})}^{2} - F_{n}F_{n-1} - {(F_{n-1})}^{2} = {(F_{n})}^{2} - F_{n-1}(F_{n} + F_{n-1}) = \)

\( = {(F_{n})}^{2} - F_{n-1}F_{n+1} = -(F_{n+1}F_{n-1} - {(F_{n})}^{2}) = -{(-1)}^{n} = {(-1)}^{n-1} \)

\section*{(6)}

\( F_{n+m} = F_{m}F_{n+1} + F_{m-1}F_{n}, n \geq 0, m \geq 1 \quad (6) \)

First, let's prove for \( m \in \mathbb{Z} \) via reverse induction.

\( (m \implies m - 1) \)

\( F_{m-1}F_{n+1} + F_{m-2}F_{n} = F_{m-1}F_{n+1} + (F_{m} - F_{m-1})F_{n} = F_{m-1}F_{n+1} + F_{m}F_{n} - F_{m-1}F_{n} = \)

\( = F_{m}F_{n} + F_{m-1}(F_{n+1} - F_{n}) = F_{m}F_{n} + F_{m-1}F_{n-1} =: A \)

If \( n - 1 \geq 0 \), we can use (6) for \( (m, n -1) \)

\( A = F_{(n-1)+m} = F_{n + (m-1)} \)

Case \( n = 0 \) we need check separately

\( A = F_{m}F_{0} + F_{m-1}F_{-1} = F_{m} \cdot 0 + F_{m-1} \cdot 1 = F_{m-1} = F_{n + (m-1)} \)

By reverse induction we proved (6) for \( n \geq 0, m \in \mathbb{Z} \)

Now, via reverse induction for \( n \) we will prove for \( n \in \mathbb{Z} \)

\( (n \implies n - 1) \)

\( F_{m}F_{n} + F_{m-1}F_{n-1} = F_{m}F_{n} + F_{m-1}(F_{n+1} - F_{n}) = F_{m}F_{n} + F_{m-1}F_{n+1} - F_{m-1}F_{n} = \)

\( = F_{m-1}F_{n+1} + (F_{m}-F_{m-1})F_{n} = F_{m-1}F_{n+1} + F_{m-2}F_{n} \overset{\textrm{Induction step for } (m-1, n)}{=} F_{n+(m-1)} = F_{(n-1) + m} \)

Now (6) is proven for \( m,n \in \mathbb{Z} \)

\section*{(14)}

\( F_{n} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}(\phi^{n} - \widehat{\phi}^{n}), n \geq 0 \quad (14) \)

Let's prove by reverse induction

\( (n \implies n - 1) \)

\( F_{n-1} = F_{n+1} - F_{n} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}(\phi^{n+1} - \widehat{\phi}^{n+1}) - \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}(\phi^{n} - \widehat{\phi}^{n}) = \)

\( = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}(\phi^{n}(\phi -1) - \widehat{\phi}^{n}(\widehat{\phi} - 1)) =: A \)

\( \phi, \widehat{\phi} \) are solutions of \( x^{2}-x-1 = 0 \)

\( x - 1 = \frac{1}{x} \)

\( \phi - 1 = \frac{1}{\phi} \)

\( \widehat{\phi} - 1 = \frac{1}{\widehat{\phi}} \)

\( A = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}(\phi^{n-1} - \widehat{\phi}^{n-1}) \)
