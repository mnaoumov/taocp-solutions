Let \( r \perp s \)

\( 0 \leq x_{1} < x_{2} < \cdots < x_{\varphi(r)} \leq r - 1 \)

\( 0 \leq y_{1} < y_{2} < \cdots < y_{\varphi(s)} \leq s - 1 \)

\( 0 \leq z_{1} < z_{2} < \cdots < z_{\varphi(rs)} \leq rs - 1 \)

\( Z := \{ z_{1}, z_{2}, \ldots, z_{\varphi(rs)} \} \)

\( x_{i} \perp r, 1 \leq i \leq \varphi(r) \)

\( y_{j} \perp s, 1 \leq j \leq \varphi(s) \)

\( z_{k} \perp (rs), 1 \leq k \leq \varphi(rs) \)

\( r \perp s \implies \exists r',s': rr' \equiv 1 \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ s), ss' \equiv 1 \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ r), \)

\( u_{ij} := (x_{i}ss' + y_{j}rr') \mod rs, 1 \leq i \leq \varphi(r) - 1, 1 \leq j \leq \varphi(s) - 1 \)

\( u_{ij} \equiv x_{i}(ss') + 0 \equiv x_{i}\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ r) \)

\( u_{ij} \equiv 0 + y_{j}(rr') \equiv y_{j}\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ s) \)

\( U := \{ u_{ij}: 1 \leq i \leq \varphi(r) - 1, 1 \leq j \leq \varphi(s) - 1 \} \)

\( \#U = \varphi(r)\varphi(s) \)

\( \forall u_{ij} \in U \)

Let's prove \( u_{ij} \perp (rs) \)

Assume \( u_{ij} \not\perp (rs) \implies \exists p \) --- prime, \( p \backslash u_{ij}, p \backslash (rs) \)

\( p \backslash (rs) \implies (p \backslash r) \lor (p \backslash s) \)

If \( p \backslash r \)

\( x_{i} ss' = u_{ij} - y_{j} rr' \implies p \backslash (x_{i} ss') \)

\( x_{i} ss' \equiv 0\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ p) \)

\( x_{i} \perp r \implies x_{i} \perp p \)

\( \implies ss' \equiv 0\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ p) \implies ss' \equiv 0\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ r) \) --- contradiction.

Similarly, if \( p \backslash s \) getting another contradiction.

\( \implies u_{ij} \perp (rs) \implies u_{ij} \in Z \)

\( \forall z_{k} \in Z \)

\( x := z_{k} \mod r, y := z_{k} \mod s \)

\( z_{k} \perp (rs) \implies z_{k} \perp r, z_{k} \perp s \implies x \perp r, y \perp s \)

\( \exists i,j: x = x_{i}, y = y_{j} \)

\( v := |z_{k}-u_{i,j}| \)

\( 0 \leq v \leq rs - 1 \)

\( v \equiv 0\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ r) \)

\( v \equiv 0\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ s) \)

\( \implies v \equiv 0\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ rs) \implies v = 0 \)

\( \implies z_{k} = u_{ij} \in U \)

\( \implies Z = U \implies \#Z = \#U \implies \varphi(rs) = \varphi(r)\varphi(s) \)
