Let \( s = \lfloor \frac{\lfloor x \rfloor +m}{n} \rfloor \)

\( s \leq \frac{\lfloor x \rfloor +m}{n} < s + 1 \)

\( sn \leq \lfloor x \rfloor + m < sn+n \)

\( sn - m \leq \lfloor x \rfloor < sn + n - m \)

\( sn - m \leq x < sn + n - m \)

\( s \leq \frac{x+m}{n} < s + 1 \)

\( \implies s = \lfloor \frac{x+m}{n} \rfloor \)

\( \lceil \frac{\lceil x \rceil + m}{n} \rceil = -\lfloor \frac{-\lceil x \rceil - m}{n} \rfloor = -\lfloor \frac{\lfloor -x \rfloor + (- m)}{n} \rfloor = -\lfloor \frac{(-x) + (- m)}{n} \rfloor = \lceil \frac{x + m}{n} \rceil \)
