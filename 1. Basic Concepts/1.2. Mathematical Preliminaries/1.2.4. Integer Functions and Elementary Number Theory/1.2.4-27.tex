\( 0 \not\perp p \)

\( \forall k, 1 \leq k \leq p - 1: k \perp p \)

\( \implies \varphi(p) = \#\{1,2,\ldots,p-1\} = p - 1 \)

Consider \( \varphi(p^{e}) \)

\( k \not\perp p^{e} \iff p \backslash k \iff k \in \{ p, 2p, \ldots, p^{e} - p \} \)

\( \varphi(p^{e}) = \#(\{0,1,\ldots,p^{e}-1\} \setminus \{ p, 2p, \ldots, p^{e} - p \}) = p^{e} - p^{e-1} \)
