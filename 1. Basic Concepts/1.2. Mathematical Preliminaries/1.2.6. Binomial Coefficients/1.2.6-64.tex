\( (46)\quad \{{{n+1} \atop m}\} = m\{{{n} \atop m}\} + \{{{n} \atop m - 1}\} \)

Let \( P_{nm} = \{ B = \{ B_{1}, \ldots B_{m} \}, B_{1}\sqcup B_{2}\sqcup \cdots \sqcup B_{m} = \{ 1, 2, \ldots, n + 1 \}, B_{i} \neq \varnothing, 1 \leq i \leq m \} \) --- partitions of \( \{ 1, 2, \ldots, n\} \) in \( m \) non-empty parts.

As order of \( B_{i} \) is not relevant, we can assume \( n \in B_{m} \)

Consider \( B \in P_{n+1,m} \)

Let

If \( B_{m} = \{ n + 1 \} \implies B' = \{ B_{1}, B_{2}, \ldots, B_{m-1} \} \in P_{n,m-1} \)

If \( B_{m} \neq \{ n + 1 \} \implies B'' = \{ B_{1}, B_{2}, \ldots, B_{m-1}, B_{m} \setminus \{ n + 1 \} \} \in P_{nm} \)

Reverse, \( \forall B \in P_{n, m-1}: B \sqcup \{ \{ n + 1 \} \} \in P_{n+1,m} \)

\( \forall B \in P_{n, m}: B^{(k)} := \{ B_{1}, B_{2}, \ldots, B_{k} \sqcup \{ n + 1 \}, \ldots, B_{m} \} \in P_{n+1,m}, 1 \leq k \leq m \)

\( \implies \#P_{n+1,m} = m \#P_{nm} + \#P_{n,m-1} \)

\( \{{0 \atop 0}\} = \#P_{00} = 1 \)

\( \{{0 \atop m}\} = \#P_{0m} = 0, m \geq 1 \)

\( \{{n \atop 0}\} = \#P_{n0} = 0, n \geq 1 \)

\( \{{n \atop m}\} = \#P_{nm}, n \geq 0, m \geq 0 \)
