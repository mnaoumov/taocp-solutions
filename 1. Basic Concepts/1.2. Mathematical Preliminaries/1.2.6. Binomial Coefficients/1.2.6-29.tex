\( (34): \sum_{k} \binom{r}{k} {(-1)}^{r-k} (b_{0} + b_{1} k + \cdots + b_{r} k^{r}) = r! b_{r} \)

\( \binom{r}{k} {(-1)}^{r-k} = \frac{r!{(-1)}^{r-k}}{k!(r-k)!} = \frac{r!}{k!\cdot(-1)(-2)\cdots(-(r-k))} = \frac{r!{(-1)}^{r-k}}{k!(r-k)!} = \frac{r!}{(k-0)(k-1)\cdots(k-(k-1))\cdot(k-(k+1))(k-(k+2))\cdots(k-r)} = \frac{r!}{\prod_{\substack{0 \leq j \leq r\\j \neq k}}(k-j)} \)

\( \sum_{k} \binom{r}{k} {(-1)}^{r-k} (b_{0} + b_{1} k + \cdots + b_{r} k^{r}) = \sum_{s=0}^{r} r! b_{s} \sum_{k=0}^{r} \frac{k^{s}}{\prod_{\substack{0 \leq j \leq r\\j \neq k}}(k-j)} =: A \)
