\( \sum_{k} \binom{r}{k}\binom{s+k}{n}{(-1)}^{r-k} = \binom{s}{n - r} \)

\( r = 0 \)

\( \binom{0}{0}\binom{s+0}{n}{(-1)}^{0-0} = \binom{s}{n-0} \)

\( (r \implies r + 1) \)

\( \sum_{k} \binom{r+1}{k}\binom{s+k}{n}{(-1)}^{r+1-k} = \sum_{k} \bigl(\binom{r}{k} + \binom{r}{k-1}\bigr)\binom{s+k}{n}{(-1)}^{r+1-k} = -\sum_{k} \binom{r}{k}\binom{s+k}{n}{(-1)}^{r-k} + \sum_{k} \binom{r}{k-1}\binom{s+k}{n}{(-1)}^{r+1-k} = \)

\( = -\binom{s}{n - r} + \sum_{k} \binom{r}{k}\binom{s+k+1}{n}{(-1)}^{r+1-k-1} = -\binom{s}{n - r} + \binom{s+1}{n - r} = \binom{s}{n - (r+1)} \)
