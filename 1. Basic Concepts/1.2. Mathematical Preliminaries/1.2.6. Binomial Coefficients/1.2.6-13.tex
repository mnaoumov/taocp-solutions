\( \sum_{k=0}^{n} \binom{r+k}{k} = \binom{r+n+1}{n} \)

\( n = 0 \)

\( \binom{r+0}{0} = \binom{r+0+1}{0} \)

\( (n \implies n + 1) \)

\begin{multline*}
\sum_{k=0}^{n+1} \binom{r+k}{k} = \sum_{k=0}^{n} \binom{r+k}{k} + \binom{r+n+1}{n+1} \\
= \binom{r+n+1}{n} + \binom{r+n+1}{n+1} = \binom{r+n+2}{n+1} = \binom{r+(n+1)+1}{n+1}
\end{multline*}
