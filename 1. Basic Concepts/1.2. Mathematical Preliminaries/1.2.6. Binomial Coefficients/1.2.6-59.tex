\( A_{0k} = \delta_{0k} = \binom{0}{k} \)

\( k > 0: A_{1k} = A_{0k} + A_{0,k-1} + \binom{1}{k} = \binom{0}{k} + \binom{0}{k - 1} + \binom{1}{k} = 2 \binom{1}{k} \)

For \( k \geq 0: A_{1k} = 2\binom{1}{k} - \binom{1}{k + 1} \)

\( k > 0: A_{2k} = A_{1k} + A_{1,k-1} + \binom{2}{k} = 2\binom{1}{k} - \binom{1}{k+1} + 2\binom{1}{k-1} - \binom{1}{k} + \binom{2}{k} = \)

\( = 2\binom{2}{k} - \binom{2}{k+1} + \binom{2}{k} = 3 \binom{2}{k} - \binom{2}{k+1} \)
