[1.2.6-22](<./1.2.6-22.md>) \( \implies LHS = RHS \) for \( (r,n-1-r+tn,t,n) \)

Repeatedly applying [1.2.6-23](<./1.2.6-23.md>) \( \implies LHS = RHS \) for \( (r,n-1-r+tn + m,t,n), m \in \mathbb{N}_0 \)

Treating \( LHS, RHS \) as polynomials of degree \( \leq n \) of \( s \), we see \( LHS = RHS \) for infinite amount of values \( s \implies LHS = RHS \) is identity.
