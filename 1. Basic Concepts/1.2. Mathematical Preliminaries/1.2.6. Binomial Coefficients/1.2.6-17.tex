\( \sum_{k} \binom{r}{k}x^{k} = {(1+x)}^{r} \)

\( \sum_{l} \binom{s}{l}x^{l} = {(1+x)}^{s} \)

\( {(1+x)}^{r+s} = \sum_{k} \binom{r}{k}x^{k} \cdot \sum_{l} \binom{s}{l}x^{l} = \sum_{k,l} \binom{r}{k} \binom{s}{l} x^{k+l} = \sum_{n} x^{n} \sum_{\substack{k,l\\k + l = n}} \binom{r}{k} \binom{s}{l} = \sum_{n} x^{n} \sum_{k} \binom{r}{k} \binom{s}{n-l} \)

\( {(1+x)}^{r+s} = \sum_{n} x^{n} \binom{r+s}{n} \)

\( \implies \binom{r+s}{n} = \sum_{k} \binom{r}{k} \binom{s}{n-l} \)
