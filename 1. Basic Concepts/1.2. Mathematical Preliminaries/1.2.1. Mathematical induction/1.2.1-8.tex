\subsubsection*{a)}

\( 1^3 = 1 \)

\( 2^3 = 3 + 5 \)

\( 3^3 = 7 + 9 + 11 \)

\( 4^3 = 13 + 15 + 17 + 19 \)

We see that each row has \( n \) summands. Consecutive odd numbers.

\( P(n): n^3 = \sum_{i=0}^{n-1}(a_n+2i) \)

\( a_n \) is the first summand.

\( a_1 = 1 \)

\( a_2=3=a_1+2 \)

\( a_3=7=a_2+4 \)

\( a_4=13=a_3+6 \)

It seems \( a_n = a_{n-1} + 2(n-1) \)

\( a_1=a_0+2\cdot 0 \implies a_0 = 1 \)

\( a_n = a_0 + 2 \cdot 0 + 2 \cdot 1 + \cdots + 2 \cdot (n-1) = 1 + 2\cdot \frac{n(n-1)}{2} = n^2-n+1 \)

\( Q(n): a_n = n^2-n+1 \)

\( a_1=1^2-1+1 = 1 \implies Q(1) \)

\( a_n = a_{n-1} + 2(n-1) = (n-1)^2-(n-1)+1+2(n-1) = n^2-2n+1-n+1+1+2n-2=n^2-n+1 \implies Q(n) \)

\( b_n = \sum_{i=0}^{n-1} (n^2-n+1+2i) \)

\( P(n): n^3 = b_n \)

\( b_1 = 1 \implies P(1) \)

Assume \( P(n) \) holds

\( b_{n+1} = \sum_{i=0}^{n} ((n+1)^2-(n+1)+1+2i) = \sum_{i=0}^{n} (n^2+2n+1-n-1+1+2i) = \sum_{i=0}^{n} (n^2+n+1+2i) = \sum_{i=0}^{n} (n^2-n+1+2i) + \sum_{i=0}^{n} 2n = \)

\( = \sum_{i=0}^{n-1} (n^2-n+1+2i) + (n^2-n+1+2n) + 2n(n+1) = b_n + (n^2+n+1) + (2n^2+2n) = b^n + (3n^2+3n+1) = n^3 + (3n^2+3n+1) = (n+1)^3 \implies P(n+1) \)

\subsubsection*{b)}

\( 1^3+2^3+\cdots+n^3 = 1 + (3+5)+(7+9+11)+\cdots+((n^2-n+1)+(n^2-n+3)+\cdots+(n^2+n-1)) \)

Right side has \( (n^2+n-1-1)/2+1 = \frac{n^2+n}{2} \) summands

Their sum \( \frac{(1+(n^2+n - 1)))\cdot\frac{(n^2+n)}{2}}{2} = (\frac{n^2+n}{2})^2=(1+2+\cdots+n)^2 \)

\subsubsection*{v2 a)}

\( 1^3 = 1 \) - sum of 1st odd number

\( 2^3 = 3 + 5 \) - sum of 2nd, 3rd odd number

\( 3^3 = 7 + 9 + 11 \) - sum of 4nd, 5th, 6th odd number

\ldots

\( n^3 \) - sum of \( a_n \)-th, \ldots, \( b_n \)-th odd number

\( b_n = 1+2+3+\cdots+(n-1)+n=\frac{n(n+1)}{2} \)

\( a_n = b_{n-1} + 1 = \frac{(n-1)n}{2} + 1 = \frac{n^2-n+2}{2} \)

\( k \)-th odd number is \( 2k-1 \)

\( n^3 \) - sum of odd numbers from \( 2a_n-1 \) to \( 2b_n-1 \)

\( n^3 = (n^2-n+1) + (n^2-n+3) + (n^2-n+5) + \cdots + (n^2+n-1) \)

Proof without math induction

\( (n^2-n+1) + (n^2-n+3) + (n^2-n+5) + \cdots + (n^2+n-1) = (n^2-n) + 1 + (n^2-n) + 3 + \cdots + (n^2-n) + (2n - 1) = (n^2-n)n + (1 + 3 + \cdots + (2n-1)) = \)

\( = n^3-n^2 + \frac{(1 + (2n-1))n}{2} = n^3-n^2 + n^2 = n^3 \)
