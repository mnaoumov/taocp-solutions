L1.\ \( y \leftarrow 1, k \leftarrow 0 \)

L2. If \( x = 0, y \) --- is the result. Stop.

L3. If \( x < \log_b{\frac{2^{k}+1}{2^{k}}}, k \leftarrow k + 1 \).\ Go to L3.

L4.\ \( x \leftarrow x - \log_b{\frac{2^{k}+1}{2^{k}}}, y \leftarrow y + (y \) shift right \( k) \).\ Go to L2.

Let binary computer's precision be \( p \implies x = \frac{a}{2^{p}}, y = \frac{c}{2^{p}}, a,c \in \mathbb{Z} \)

\( d_{k} := \frac{2^{k}+1}{2^{k}}, e_{k} := \lfloor2^{p}\log_b{d_{k}}\rfloor \)

L1.\ \( c \leftarrow 2^{p}, k \leftarrow 1 \)

L2. If \( a = 0, y = \frac{c}{2^{p}} \) --- is the result. Stop.

L3. If \( a < e_{k}, k \leftarrow k + 1 \).\ Go to L3.

L4.\ \( a \leftarrow a - e_{k}, c \leftarrow c + \lfloor\frac{c}{2^{k}}\rfloor \).\ Go to L2.

\( c' = c + \lfloor\frac{c}{2^{k}}\rfloor = c + \frac{c}{2^{k}} - \{ \frac{c}{2^{k}} \} = cd_{k} - \{ \frac{c}{2^{k}} \} = cd_{k} (1 - \frac{\{ \frac{c}{2^{k}} \}}{cd_{k}}) \)

\( f := |x_{0}-x - \log_{b}y| \)

\( f_{0} = 0 \)

\( f'=|x_{0}-x' - \log_b{y'}| = |x_{0}-x + x - x' - \log_b{y} - \log_b{\frac{y'}{y}} | \leq f + | \frac{a-a'}{2^{p}} - \log_b{\frac{c'}{c}} | \leq f + | \frac{e_{k}}{2^{p}} - \log_b{(d_{k}(1 - \frac{\{ \frac{c}{2^{k}} \}}{cd_{k}}))} | \)

\( \frac{e_{k}}{2^{p}} = \frac{2^{p}\log_b{d_{k}} - \{2^{p}\log_b{d_{k}}\}}{2^{p}} = \log_b{d_{k}} - \frac{\{2^{p}\log_b{d_{k}}\}}{2^{p}} \)

\( f' \leq f + \frac{\{2^{p}\log_b{d_{k}}\}}{2^{p}} + |\log_b{(1 - \frac{\{ \frac{c}{2^{k}} \}}{cd_{k}})}| \leq f + \frac{1}{2^{p}} + \frac{|\ln(1 - \frac{\{ \frac{c}{2^{k}} \}}{cd_{k}})|}{|\ln{b}|} \leq f + \frac{1}{2^{p}} + \frac{1}{|\ln{b}|} \cdot \frac{\{ \frac{c}{2^{k}} \}}{cd_{k}} \leq f + \frac{1}{2^{p}} + \frac{1}{|\ln{b}|} \cdot \frac{1}{2^{p} \cdot 1} = f + \frac{1}{2^{p}} (1 + \frac{1}{|\ln{b}|}) \)

\( \implies f \leq \frac{1}{2^{p}} (1 + \frac{1}{|\ln{b}|}) \cdot C_{L4} \)

By mean theorem \( |b^{s}-b^{t}| = b^{u}\ln{b}|s-t|, u \in (s,t) \)

\( |b^{x_{0}}-y| = |b^{x_{0}}-b^{\log_b{y}}| = b^{u}|\ln{b}||x_{0}-\log_b{y}| =:A \)

\( x_{0} < 1 \implies b^{u} < b \)

\( A \leq b|\ln{b}| \frac{1}{2^{p}} (1 + \frac{1}{|\ln{b}|}) \cdot C_{L4} \)
