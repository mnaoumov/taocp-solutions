\( x(1-\delta) \leq 10^{n}\cdot x_{0}' \leq x(1+\epsilon) \)

Let's prove by induction:

\( {(\frac{x(1-\delta)}{10^{n+\frac{b_{1}}{2^{1}}+\frac{b_{2}}{2^{2}}+\cdots+\frac{b_{k}}{2^{k}}}})}^{2^{k}} \leq x_{k}' \leq {(\frac{x(1+\epsilon)}{10^{n+\frac{b_{1}}{2^{1}}+\frac{b_{2}}{2^{2}}+\cdots+\frac{b_{k}}{2^{k}}}})}^{2^{k}} \)

\( \log'{x} = r_{k}:= n+\frac{b_{1}}{2^{1}}+\frac{b_{2}}{2^{2}}+\cdots+\frac{b_{k}}{2^{k}} \)

\( k = 0 \), by definition

\( (k \implies k + 1) \)

\( {(\frac{x(1-\delta)}{10^{r_{k}}})}^{2^{k+1}} \leq {(x_{k}')}^{2} \leq {(\frac{x(1+\epsilon)}{10^{r_{k}}})}^{2^{k+1}} \)

\( {(\frac{x(1-\delta)}{10^{r_{k}}})}^{2^{k+1}} (1-\delta) \leq {(x_{k}')}^{2} (1-\delta) \leq y_{k+1} \leq {(x_{k}')}^{2} (1+\epsilon) \leq {(\frac{x(1+\epsilon)}{10^{r_{k}}})}^{2^{k+1}} (1+\epsilon) \)

\( 1 \leq y_{k+1} < 100 \)

\( 1 \leq y_{k+1} \leq {(\frac{x(1+\epsilon)}{10^{r_{k}}})}^{2^{k+1}} (1+\epsilon) \)

\( {(\frac{x(1-\delta)}{10^{r_{k}}})}^{2^{k+1}} (1-\delta) \leq y_{k+1} < 100 \)

\( 0 \leq 2^{k+1} (\log_{10}{x} + \log_{10}{(1+\epsilon)} - r_{k}) + \log_{10}{(1+\epsilon)} \)

\( \implies r_{k} \leq \log_{10}{x} + \log_{10}{(1+\epsilon)}(1+2^{-k-1}) \leq \log_{10}{x} + 2\log_{10}{(1+\epsilon)} \)

\( 2^{k+1} (\log_{10}{x} + \log_{10}{(1-\delta)} - r_{k}) + \log_{10}{(1-\delta)} < 2 \)

\( \implies r_{k} > \log_{10}{x} + \log_{10}{(1-\delta)}(1+2^{-k-1}) - \frac{1}{2^{k}} > \log_{10}{x} + 2\log_{10}(1-\delta) - \frac{1}{2^{k}} \)
