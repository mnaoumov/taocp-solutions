\( = \sum_{s=2}^{n+1}\sum_{l=1}^{s-1}(\frac{1}{l} + \frac{1}{s-l})\frac{1}{s} = \sum_{s=2}^{n+1}\frac{1}{s}\sum_{l=1}^{s-1}(\frac{1}{l} + \frac{1}{s-l}) = \sum_{s=2}^{n+1}\frac{2H_{s-1}}{s} = \sum_{s=2}^{n+1}\frac{2H_{s-1}}{s} + \frac{2H_{0}}{1} = \sum_{s=1}^{n+1}\frac{2H_{s-1}}{s} = \)

\( = 2 \sum_{s=1}^{n+1} \frac{H_{s} - \frac{1}{s}}{s} = 2 (\sum_{s=1}^{n+1} \frac{H_{s}}{s} - \sum_{s=1}^{n+1} \frac{1}{s^{2}}) = 2(\sum_{s=1}^{n+1} \frac{H_{s}}{s} - H_{n+1}^{(2)}) \)
