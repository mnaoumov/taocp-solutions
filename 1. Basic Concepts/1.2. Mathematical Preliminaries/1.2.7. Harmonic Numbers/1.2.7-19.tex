\( H_0 = 0 \in \mathbb{Z} \)

\( H_1 = 1 \in \mathbb{Z} \)

\( \forall n \geq 2 \exists k\geq 1: 2^k \leq n < 2^{k+1} \)

\( 2^{k-1}H_n = \sum_{i=1}^n \frac{2^{k-1}}{i} = \frac{1}{2} + \sum_{\substack{1 \leq i \leq n\\ i \neq 2^k}} \frac{2^{k-1}}{i} \)

\( 2^{k-1}H_n - \frac{1}{2} = \frac{a}{b}, 2 \not\mid b \)

Assume \( H_n = m \in \mathbb{Z} \)

\( 2^{k-1}H_n - \frac{1}{2} = \frac{2m-1}{2} = \frac{a}{b} \)

\( (2m-1)b = 2a \implies 2 \mid (2m-1)b \implies \perp \)
