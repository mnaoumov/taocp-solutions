\( H_{1}^{(r)} = 1 \)

\( H_{2^{k+1}}^{(r)} - H_{2^{k}}^{(r)} = \sum_{j=2^{k}+1}^{2^{k+1}} \frac{1}{j^{r}} \leq \sum_{j=2^{k}+1}^{2^{k+1}} \frac{1}{2^{kr}} = 2^{k} \cdot \frac{1}{2^{kr}} = \frac{1}{2^{k(r-1)}} \)

\( H_{2^{m}}^{(r)} - H_{1}^{(r)} = \sum_{k=0}^{m-1} (H_{2^{k+1}}-H_{2^{k}}) \leq \sum_{k=0}^{m-1}{(\frac{1}{2^{r-1}})}^{k} = \frac{1-{(\frac{1}{2^{r-1}})}^{m}}{1 - \frac{1}{2^{r-1}}} \leq \frac{1}{1 - \frac{1}{2^{r-1}}} = \frac{2^{r-1}}{2^{r-1} - 1} \)

\( H_{2^{m}}^{(r)} \leq 1 + \frac{2^{r-1}}{2^{r-1} - 1} = \frac{2^{r} - 1}{2^{r-1} - 1} \)
