\( T(m, n) = H_m + H_n − H_{mn} \)

\section*{a}

\( T(m+1,n) - T(m,n) = H_{m+1} + H_n - H_{(m+1)n} - H_m - H_n + H_{mn} = (H_{m+1} - H_m) - (H_{mn+n} + H_{mn}) = \)

\( = \frac{1}{m+1} - (\frac{1}{mn+1} + \frac{1}{mn+2} + \cdots + \frac{1}{mn+n}) \leq \frac{1}{m+1} - n \cdot \frac{1}{mn+n} = 0 \)

Symmetrically, \( T(m, n + 1) - T(m,n) \leq 0 \)

\section*{b}

\( T(\cdot, n), T(m, \cdot) \downarrow \implies \max T(m,n) = T(1,1) = 1 \)

\( \nexists \min T(m,n) \)

\( \inf T(m,n) = \lim_{m,n \to \infty} T(m,n) = \lim_{m,n \to \infty} (\ln m + \gamma + \ln{n} + \gamma - \ln{mn} -\gamma) = \gamma \)
