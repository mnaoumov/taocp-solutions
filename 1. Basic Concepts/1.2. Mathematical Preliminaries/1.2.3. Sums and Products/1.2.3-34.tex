\( S = \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{\prod_{1 \leq r \leq n, r \neq m} (x + k - r)}{\prod_{1 \leq r \leq n, r \neq k} (k - r)} \)

\( x_{k} := k \)

\( f(t) := \prod_{1 \leq r \leq n, r \neq m} (x + t - r) = \sum_{i=0}^{n-1} a_{i}t^{i} \)

\( p(t) = \prod_{1 \leq r \leq n} (t-x_{r}) \)

\( \prod_{1 \leq r \leq n, r \neq k} (x_{k} - x_{r}) = p'(x_{k}) \)

\( S = \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{f(x_{k})}{p'(x_{k})} = \sum_{i=0}^{n-1} a_{i} \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{x_{k}^{i}}{p'(x_{k})} \)

By \href{./1.2.3-33.pdf}{1.2.3\mbox{-}33}:

\( S = a_{0} \cdot 0 + a_{1} \cdot 0 + \cdots + a_{n-2} \cdot 0 + a_{n-1} \cdot 1 = a_{n-1} \)

\( f(t) = t^{n-1} + g(t), \deg d \leq n - 1 \implies a_{n-1} = 1 \)

\( S = 1 \)
