Let \( n \) --- amount of \( j: R(j) \)

By condition \( n \) is finite

\( n = 0 \)

\( \log_{b} \prod_{R(j)} a_{j} = \log_{b} 1 = 0 = \sum_{R(j)} \log_{b} a_{j} \)

\( (n \implies n + 1) \)

Let \( k \) be some value such as \( R(k) \)

\( S(j) := \{ j = k \}, T(j) := \{ R(j) \land j \neq k \} \)

\( R(j) = \{ S(j) \lor T(j) \} \implies \prod_{R(j)} a_{j} = \prod_{S(j) \lor T(j)} a_{j} \)

\( S(j) \land T(j) \) --- none \( \implies \prod_{S(j) \land T(j)} a_{j} = 1 \)

\( \prod_{R(j)} a_{j} = \prod_{S(j) \lor T(j)} a_{j} \cdot \prod_{S(j) \land T(j)} a_{j} = \prod_{S(j)} a_{j} \cdot \prod_{T(j)} a_{j} = a_{k} \cdot \prod_{T(j)} a_{j} \)

\( \log_{b}\prod_{R(j)} a_{j} = \log_b{a_{k}} + \log_{b}\prod_{T(j)} a_{j} \)

\( \#\{ j: T(j) \} = \#\{ j: T(j) \} - 1 = n \)

\( \implies \log_{b}\prod_{T(j)} a_{j} = \sum_{T(j)} \log_b{a_{j}} \)

\( \log_b{a_{k}} = \sum_{S(j)} \log_b{a_{j}} \)

\( \log_{b}\prod_{R(j)} a_{j} = \sum_{S(j)} \log_b{a_{j}} + \sum_{T(j)} \log_b{a_{j}} = \sum_{S(j) \lor T(j)} \log_b{a_{j}} + \sum_{S(j) \land T(j)} \log_b{a_{j}} = \sum_{R(j)} \log_b{a_{j}} + 0 = \sum_{R(j)} \log_b{a_{j}} \)
