Induction by \( n \)

\( n = 1 \)

\( \prod_{j=1}^{1} (1-a_{j}) = 1-a_{1} \geq 1 - \sum_{j=1}^{1} a_{j} \)

\( (n \implies n+1) \)

\( \prod_{j=1}^{n+1} (1-a_{j}) = (1-a_{n+1}) \cdot \prod_{j=1}^{n} (1-a_{j}) \geq (1-a_{n+1}) \cdot (1 - \sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{j}) = 1 - \sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{j} - a_{n+1} + \sum_{j=1}^{n} a_{j}a_{n+1} \geq 1 - \sum_{j=1}^{n+1} a_{j} \)
