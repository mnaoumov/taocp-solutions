\section*{\( i < j \)}

Let \( r(k) \) --- index of the \( k \)-th row, after removing \( i \)-th row

Let \( c(l) \) --- index of the \( l \)-th row, after removing \( j \)-th column

\( r(k) = k, 1 \leq k \leq i - 1 \)

\( r(k) = k - 1, i + 1 \leq k \leq n \)

\( c(l) = l, 1 \leq l \leq j - 1 \)

\( c(l) = l - 1, j + 1 \leq l \leq n \)

\( r(j) = j - 1, r(j+1) = j, \ldots, r(n) = n - 1 \)

\( c(i) = i, c(i+1) = i + 1, \ldots, c(j-1) = j - 1, c(j+1) = j, \ldots, c(n) = n - 1 \)

Let's reorder rows and columns to put \( r(j) \)-th row to \( n-1 \) row, and \( c(i) \)-th column to \( n-1 \)-column

For that you need,

\( R_{j-1} \leftrightarrow R_{j}, R_{j} \leftrightarrow R_{j+1}, \ldots, R_{n-2} \leftrightarrow R_{n-1} \)

\( C_{i} \leftrightarrow C_{i+1}, C_{i+1} \leftrightarrow C_{i+2}, \ldots, C_{n-2} \leftrightarrow C_{n-1} \)

In total you have to make \( n-j \) row swaps and \( n-i+1 \) column swaps

\( D(n-1,x,y) := \left|\begin{matrix}x + y & y & y & \cdots & y & y \\ y & x + y & y & \cdots & y & y \\ y & y & x + y & \cdots & y & y \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots & \vdots \\ y & y & y & \cdots & x+y & y \\ y & y & y & \cdots & y & y \end{matrix}\right| \)

\( \hat{C}_{ij} = {(-1)}^{n-j+n-i+1} D(n-1, x, y) \)

\( D(n-1,x,y) \overset{R_{1} \leftarrow R_{1} - R_{n-1}}{=} \left|\begin{matrix}x  & 0 & 0 & \cdots & 0 & 0 \\ y & x + y & y & \cdots & y & y \\ y & y & x + y & \cdots & y & y \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots & \vdots \\ y & y & y & \cdots & x+y & y \\ y & y & y & \cdots & y & y \end{matrix}\right| \overset{\text{Expand by}\ R_{1}}{=} xD(n-2,x,y) \)

\( D(1,x,y) = |y| = y \)

By induction \( D(n-1,x,y) = x^{n-2}y \)

\( \hat{C}_{ij} = {(-1)}^{i+j+1}x^{n-2}y \)

\( b_{ij} = \frac{{(-1)}^{i+j}}{x^{n-1}(x+ny)} {(-1)}^{i+j+1}x^{n-2}y = \frac{-y}{x(x+ny)} \)
