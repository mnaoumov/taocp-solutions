Let \( \{ A(j), B(j), C(j), D(j) \} = \{ R(j), S(j), R(j)\ \textrm{or}\ S(j), R(j)\ \textrm{and}\ S(j) \} \)

Series with \( A(j), B(j), C(j) \) - converge

\( \sum_{A(j)} a_j = \lim_{n\to\infty} \sum_{A(j,n)} a_j + \lim_{n\to\infty} \sum_{A(j,-n)} a_j \)

\( \sum_{B(j)} a_j = \lim_{n\to\infty} \sum_{B(j,n)} a_j + \lim_{n\to\infty} \sum_{B(j,-n)} a_j \)

\( \sum_{C(j)} a_j = \lim_{n\to\infty} \sum_{C(j,n)} a_j + \lim_{n\to\infty} \sum_{C(j,-n)} a_j \)

We choose the order, such that

\( \sum_{A(j,n)} a_j + \sum_{B(j,n)} a_j = \sum_{C(j,n)} a_j + \sum_{D(j,n)} a_j \)

\( \sum_{A(j,-n)} a_j + \sum_{B(j,-n)} a_j = \sum_{C(j,-n)} a_j + \sum_{D(j,-n)} a_j \)

\( \implies \exists \lim_{n\to\infty} \sum_{D(j,n)} a_j = \lim_{n\to\infty}(\sum_{A(j,n)} a_j + \sum_{B(j,n)} a_j - \sum_{C(j,n)} a_j) \)

\( \exists \lim_{n\to\infty} \sum_{D(j,-n)} a_j = \lim_{n\to\infty}(\sum_{A(j,-n)} a_j + \sum_{B(j,-n)} a_j - \sum_{C(j,-n)} a_j) \)

\( \implies \exists \sum_{D(j)} a_j = \sum_{A(j)} a_j + \sum_{B(j)} a_j - \sum_{C(j)} a_j \)
