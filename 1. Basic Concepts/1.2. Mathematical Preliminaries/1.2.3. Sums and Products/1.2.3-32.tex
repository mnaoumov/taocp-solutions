Induction by \( n \).

\( n = 1 \)

\( \prod_{j=1}^n \sum_{i=1}^m a_{ij} = \sum_{i=1}^m a_{i1} = \sum_{1 \leq i_1 \leq m} a_{i_1 1} \)

\( (n \implies n + 1) \)

\( \prod_{j=1}^{n+1} \sum_{i=1}^m a_{ij} = \prod_{j=1}^{n} \sum_{i=1}^m a_{ij} \cdot \sum_{i=1}^m a_{i,n+1} = (\sum_{1 \leq i_1, i_2, \ldots, i_n \leq m} a_{i_1 1}a_{i_2 2} \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot  a_{i_n n}) \cdot \sum_{i_{n+1}=1}^m a_{i_{n+1},n+1} = \sum_{1 \leq i_1, i_2, \ldots, i_n, i_{n+1} \leq m} a_{i_1 1}a_{i_2 2} \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot a_{i_n n} a_{i_{n+1},n+1} \)
