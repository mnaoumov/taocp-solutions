Induction by \( n \).

\( n = 1 \)

\( \prod_{j=1}^{n} \sum_{i=1}^{m} a_{ij} = \sum_{i=1}^{m} a_{i1} = \sum_{1 \leq i_{1} \leq m} a_{i_{1} 1} \)

\( (n \implies n + 1) \)

\( \prod_{j=1}^{n+1} \sum_{i=1}^{m} a_{ij} = \prod_{j=1}^{n} \sum_{i=1}^{m} a_{ij} \cdot \sum_{i=1}^{m} a_{i,n+1} = (\sum_{1 \leq i_{1}, i_{2}, \ldots, i_{n} \leq m} a_{i_{1} 1}a_{i_{2} 2} \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot  a_{i_{n} n}) \cdot \sum_{i_{n+1}=1}^{m} a_{i_{n+1},n+1} = \sum_{1 \leq i_{1}, i_{2}, \ldots, i_{n}, i_{n+1} \leq m} a_{i_{1} 1}a_{i_{2} 2} \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot a_{i_{n} n} a_{i_{n+1},n+1} \)
