\( A = \sum_{1 \leq j < k \leq n} (u_j-u_k)(v_j-v_k) \)

\( a_j := u_j, b_j := 1, x_j := v_j, y_j := 1 \)

Applying Binet formula ([1.2.3-30](<./1.2.3-30.md>)):

\( A = \sum_{1\leq j < k \leq n} (a_jb_k-a_kb_j)(x_jy_k-x_ky_j) = (\sum_{j=1}^n a_jx_j)(\sum_{j=1}^n b_jy_j) - (\sum_{j=1}^n a_jy_j)(\sum_{j=1}^n b_jx_j) = \)

\( = n \sum_{j=1}^n u_jv_j - (\sum_{j=1}^n u_j)(\sum_{j=1}^n v_j) \)
