\( w := e^{\frac{2 \pi i}{m}} \)

\( w^m = 1 \)

\( (1+w^j)^n w^{-jk} = \sum_{s} \binom{n}{s} w^{j(s-k)} \)

\( \sum_{j=0}^{m-1} (1+w^j)^n w^{-jk} = \sum_{s} \binom{n}{s} \sum_{j=0}^{m-1} w^{j(s-k)} = A \)

\( s-k = mq + r, 0 \leq r \leq m - 1 \)

\( A = \sum_{r=0}^{m-1} \sum_{q} \binom{n}{k + mq + r} \sum_{j=0}^{m-1} w^{j(mq+r)} = \sum_{r=0}^{m-1} \sum_{q} \binom{n}{k + mq + r} \sum_{j=0}^{m-1} (w^m)^{jq}w^{jr} = \)

\( = \sum_{r=0}^{m-1} \sum_{q} \binom{n}{k + mq + r} \sum_{j=0}^{m-1} w^{jr} = \sum_{q} \binom{n}{k + mq + 0} \sum_{j=0}^{m-1} w^{j \cdot 0} + \sum_{r=1}^{m-1} \sum_{q} \binom{n}{k + mq + r} \sum_{j=0}^{m-1} w^{jr} = \)

\( = m \sum_{q} \binom{n}{k + mq} + \sum_{r=1}^{m-1} \sum_{q} \binom{n}{k + mq + r} \frac{w^{mr} - 1}{w^r - 1} = m \sum_{q} \binom{n}{k + mq} \)

\( \sum_q \binom{n}{k+mq} = \frac{1}{m} \sum_{j=0}^{m-1} (w^{-\frac{j}{2}}+w^{\frac{j}{2}})^n w^{\frac{j(n-2k)}{2}} =: B \)

\( w^{-\frac{j}{2}} + w^{-\frac{j}{2}} = e^{\frac{\pi i j}{m}} + e^{-\frac{\pi i j}{m}} = 2 \cos \frac{\pi j}{m} \)

\( B = \frac{1}{m} \sum_{j=0}^{m-1} (2 \cos \frac{\pi j}{m})^n e^{\frac{\pi i j(n-2k)}{m}} \)

\( B \in \mathbb{R} \implies B = \Re B = \frac{1}{m} \sum_{j=0}^{m-1} (2 \cos \frac{\pi j}{m})^n \cos \frac{\pi j(n-2k)}{m} \)
