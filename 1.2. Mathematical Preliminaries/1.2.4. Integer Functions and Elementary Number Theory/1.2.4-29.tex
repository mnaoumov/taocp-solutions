Let \( r \perp s \)

\subsubsection*{(a)}

\( f(rs) = (rs)^c=r^cs^c = f(r)f(s) \)

\subsubsection*{(b)}

If \( f(r) = 0 \implies \exists k > 1: k^2 \backslash r \implies k^2 \backslash (rs) \implies f(rs) = 0 = f(r)f(s) \)

Similarly if \( f(s) = 0 \implies f(rs) = 0 = f(r)f(s) \)

If \( f(r) \neq 0, f(s) \neq 0 \implies f(r)=f(s)=1 \)

Assume \( f(rs) = 0 \implies \exists k > 1: k^2 \backslash (rs) \)

\( \exists p \) - prime, \( p \backslash k \implies p^2 \backslash (rs) \)

\( rs \equiv 0 \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ p^2) \)

If we assume \( r \perp p^2 \implies s \equiv 0 \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ p^2) \implies r(s) = 0 \) - contradiction.

\( \implies r \not\perp p^2 \implies p \backslash r \)

Similarly, \( p \backslash s \)

\( \implies r \not\perp s \) - contradiction

Therefore \( f(rs) = 1 = f(r)f(s) \)

\subsubsection*{(c)}

\( f(n) = c^k \)

\( f(1) = c^0 = 1 \)

Let \( r = p_1^{a_1} p_2^{a_2} \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot p_d^{a_d}, p_1 < p_2 < \cdots < p_d \)

\( s = q_1^{b_1} q_2^{b_2} \cdots q_e^{b_e}, q_1 < q_2 < \cdots < q_e \)

\( p_j, q_j \) - prime

\( f(r) = c^d, f(s)=c^e \)

\( r \perp s \implies p_i \neq q_j, 1 \leq i \leq d, 1 \leq j \leq e \)

\( rs = p_1^{a_1} p_2^{a_2} \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot p_d^{a_d} \cdot q_1^{b_1} q_2^{b_2} \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot q_e^{b_e} \)

\( f(rs) = c^{d+e} = c^d \cdot c^e = f(r)f(s) \)

\subsubsection*{(d)}

Let \( g,h \) - multiplicative.

\( f(rs) = g(rs)h(rs) = g(r)g(s)h(r)h(s) = (g(r)h(r))\cdot(g(s)h(s)) = f(r)f(s) \)
