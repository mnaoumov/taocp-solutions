# 1.2.6-7

${} \binom{n}{k} = \frac{n-k+1}{k} \binom{n}{k-1} {}$

${} \binom{n}{k} \geq \binom{n}{k-1} \iff \frac{n-k+1}{k} \geq 1 \iff n - k + 1 \geq k \iff k \leq \frac{n+1}{2} \iff k \leq \lfloor\frac{n+1}{2}\rfloor {}$

${} k_{max} = \lfloor\frac{n+1}{2}\rfloor {}$
