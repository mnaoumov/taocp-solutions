$x + y = 0$

$(x+y)^n = \delta_{n0}$

$n = 0$

$RHS = \binom{0}{0} x(x-0z)^{-1}(y+0z)^0=1 = \delta_{n0} = LHS$

$n > 0$

$RHS = \sum_k \binom{n}{k}x(x-kz)^{k-1}(-x+kz)^{n-k} = x(x-kz)^{n-1} \sum_{k} \binom{n}{k} (-1)^{n-k} = 0 = LHS$
