# 1.2.6-21

LHS is a polynomial of ${} s {}$ of degree ${} \leq n {}$. RHS is a polynomial of degree ${} m + n {}$. The equality is for ${} 0 \leq s \leq n {}$ - ${} n + 1 {}$ different values. Not enough to claim the identity (requires ${} n + m + 1 {}$ different values).
