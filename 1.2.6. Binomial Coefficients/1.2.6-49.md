# 1.2.6-49

${} (1+x)^r = \sum_{n} \binom{r}{n}x^n {}$

${} (1-x^2)^r = \sum_{n} \binom{r}{n} (-x^2)^n {}$

${} (1-x)^{-r} = \sum_{n} \binom{-r}{n} (-x)^n {}$

${} \binom{r}{n} = \sum_k \binom{r}{k}\binom{-r}{n-2k}(-1)^{n-k} {}$
