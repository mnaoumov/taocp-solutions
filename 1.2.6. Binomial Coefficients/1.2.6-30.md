# 1.2.6-30

${} \sum_{k} \binom{n+k}{m+2k}\binom{2k}{k}\frac{(-1)^k}{k+1} =: A {}$

${} \binom{2k}{k} = (-1)^k\binom{-1-k}{k} {}$

${} \binom{n+k}{m+2k} = \binom{n+k}{n-m-k} = \binom{(2n-m) - 1\cdot((n-m)-k)}{(n-m)-k} {}$

${} A = \sum_k \binom{-1-k}{k}\binom{(2n-m) - 1\cdot((n-m)-k)}{(n-m)-k}\frac{-1}{-1-k} =: B {}$

${} r = -1, t = 1, s = 2n-m, n = n-m {}$

${} B \overset{(34)}{=} \binom{(-1)+(2n-m)-1\cdot(n-m)}{n-m} = \binom{n - 1}{n-m} = \binom{n-1}{m-1} {}$
