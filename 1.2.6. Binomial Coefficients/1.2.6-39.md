# 1.2.6-39

${} \sum_{k} [{n \atop k}]x^k = x^{\overline{n}} {}$

${} \sum_{k} [{n \atop k}] = 1^{\overline{n}} = n! {}$

${} \sum_{k} [{n \atop k}](-1)^k = (-1)^{\overline{n}} = \delta_{n0} - \delta_{n1} {}$
