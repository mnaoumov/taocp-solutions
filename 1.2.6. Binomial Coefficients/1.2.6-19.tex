$\sum_k \binom{r}{k}\binom{s+k}{n}(-1)^{r-k} = \binom{s}{n - r}$

$r = 0$

$\binom{0}{0}\binom{s+0}{n}(-1)^{0-0} = \binom{s}{n-0}$

$(r \implies r + 1)$

$\sum_k \binom{r+1}{k}\binom{s+k}{n}(-1)^{r+1-k} = \sum_k \bigl(\binom{r}{k} + \binom{r}{k-1}\bigr)\binom{s+k}{n}(-1)^{r+1-k} = -\sum_k \binom{r}{k}\binom{s+k}{n}(-1)^{r-k} + \sum_k \binom{r}{k-1}\binom{s+k}{n}(-1)^{r+1-k} =$

$= -\binom{s}{n - r} + \sum_k \binom{r}{k}\binom{s+k+1}{n}(-1)^{r+1-k-1} = -\binom{s}{n - r} + \binom{s+1}{n - r} = \binom{s}{n - (r+1)}$
