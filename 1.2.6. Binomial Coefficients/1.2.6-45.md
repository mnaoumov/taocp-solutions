# 1.2.6-45

${} A := \lim_{r \to \infty} \overbrace{\frac{\binom{r}{k}}{r^k}}^{A_r} {}$

${} A_r = \frac{\Gamma(r+1)}{\Gamma(k+1)\Gamma(r-k+1) r^k} = \frac{1}{\Gamma(k+1)} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{2 \pi r} (\frac{r}{e})^r (1 + o(1))}{\sqrt{2 \pi (r-k)} (\frac{r-k}{e})^{r-k} r^k (1 + o(1))} = {}$

${} = \frac{1}{\Gamma(k+1)} \cdot \sqrt{\frac{r}{r-k}} (\frac{r}{r-k})^{r-k} e^-k (1+o(1)) = \frac{e^{-k}}{\Gamma(k+1)} ((1 + \frac{1}{\frac{r-k}{k}})^{\frac{r-k}{k}})^k (1+o(1)) {}$

${} A = \frac{1}{\Gamma(k+1)} {}$
