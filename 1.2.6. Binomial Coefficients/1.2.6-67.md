# 1.2.6-67

[1.2.5-24](<../1.2.5. Permutations and Factorials/1.2.5-24.md>) ${} \implies \frac{n^n}{e^{n-1}} \leq n! {}$

${} \binom{n}{k} = \frac{n(n-1)(n-2)\cdots(n-k+1)}{k!} \leq \frac{n^k}{k!} \leq \frac{n^k e^{k-1}}{k^k} = \frac{1}{e} \cdot (\frac{ne}{k})^k \leq (\frac{ne}{k})^k {}$
