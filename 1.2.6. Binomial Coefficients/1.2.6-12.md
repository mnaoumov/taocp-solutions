# 1.2.6-12

From [1.2.6-11](<./1.2.6-11.md>), ${} (n-k) + k {}$ doesn't have any carries by adding in ${} 2 {}$-ary number system.

${} \iff {}$ all 2-digits of ${} n {}$ are ${} 1 {}$.

${} \iff n = 2^k - 1 {}$
