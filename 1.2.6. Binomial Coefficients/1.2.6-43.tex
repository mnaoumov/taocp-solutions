$B(\frac{1}{2}, \frac{1}{2}) = \int_0^1 t^{-\frac{1}{2}}(1-t)^{-\frac{1}{2}}dt = \int_0^1 \frac{dt}{\sqrt{t(1-t)}} = \int_0^1 \frac{dt}{\sqrt{\frac{1}{4}- (t-\frac{1}{2})^2}} = \arcsin \frac{t-\frac{1}{2}}{\frac{1}{2}} \Bigl|_0^1 = \arcsin (2t-1) \Bigl|_0^1 = \arcsin 1 - \arcsin (-1) = \pi$

$B(\frac{1}{2}, \frac{1}{2}) = \frac{\Gamma(\frac{1}{2}) \Gamma(\frac{1}{2})}{\Gamma(1)} = (\Gamma(\frac{1}{2}))^2 \implies \Gamma(\frac{1}{2}) = \sqrt{\pi}$
