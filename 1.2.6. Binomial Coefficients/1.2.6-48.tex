\( \sum_k \binom{n}{k} \frac{(-1)^k}{k+x} = \frac{n!}{x(x+1)\cdots(x+n)} \)

\( n = 0 \)

\( \binom{0}{0} \frac{(-1)^0}{0+x} = \frac{0!}{x} \)

\( (n \implies n + 1) \)

\( \sum_k \binom{n+1}{k} \frac{(-1)^k}{k+x} = \sum_k \binom{n}{k} \frac{(-1)^k}{k+x} + \sum_k \binom{n}{k-1} \frac{(-1)^k}{k+x} = \frac{n!}{x(x+1)\cdots(x+n)} + \sum_k \binom{n}{k}\frac{(-1)^{k+1}}{k+1+x} = \)

\( = \frac{n!}{x(x+1)\cdots(x+n)} - \sum_k \binom{n}{k}\frac{(-1)^{k}}{k+(x+1)} = \frac{n!}{x(x+1)\cdots(x+n)} - \frac{n!}{(x+1)\cdots(x+n+1)} = \)

\( = \frac{n!}{(x+1)(x+2)\cdots(x+n)} (\frac{1}{x} - \frac{1}{x+n+1}) = \frac{n!}{(x+1)(x+2)\cdots(x+n)} \cdot \frac{n+1}{x(x+n+1)} = \frac{(n+1)!}{x(x+1)\cdots(x+(n+1))} \)
