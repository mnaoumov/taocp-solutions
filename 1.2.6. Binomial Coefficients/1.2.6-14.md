# 1.2.6-14

${} x^4 = \sum_{m}\{{4\atop m} \}x^{\underline{m}} = x^{\underline{1}} + 7 x^{\underline{2}} + 6 x^{\underline{3}} + x^{\underline{4}} = \binom{x}{1} + 14 \binom{x}{2} + 36 \binom{3}{1} + 24 \binom{x}{4} {}$

${} \sum_{k=0}^n k^4 = \binom{n+1}{2} + 14 \binom{n+1}{3} + 36 \binom{n+1}{4} + 24 \binom{n+1}{5} {}$
