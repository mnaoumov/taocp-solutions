# 1.2.6-52

${} LHS = ((-1) + 1)^{-1} = \infty {}$

${} RHS = \sum_{k} \binom{-1}{k}(-1)(-1-k\cdot 1)^{k-1}(1+k \cdot 1)^{-1-k} = \sum_{k \geq 0} (-1)^k (-1)^{k} (1+k)^{k-1}(1+k)^{-1-k} = \sum_{k \geq 0} \frac{1}{(1+k)^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6} \neq LHS {}$
