$\sum_k \binom{r}{k}x^k = (1+x)^r$

$\sum_l \binom{s}{l}x^l = (1+x)^s$

$(1+x)^{r+s} = \sum_k \binom{r}{k}x^k \cdot \sum_l \binom{s}{l}x^l = \sum_{k,l} \binom{r}{k} \binom{s}{l} x^{k+l} = \sum_n x^n \sum_{\substack{k,l\\k + l = n}} \binom{r}{k} \binom{s}{l} = \sum_n x^n \sum_k \binom{r}{k} \binom{s}{n-l}$

$(1+x)^{r+s} = \sum_n x^n \binom{r+s}{n}$

$\implies \binom{r+s}{n} = \sum_k \binom{r}{k} \binom{s}{n-l}$
