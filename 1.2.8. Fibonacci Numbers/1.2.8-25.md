# 1.2.8-25

${} F_n = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}(\phi^n - \hat{\phi}^n) {}$

${} \phi^n = (\frac{1+\sqrt{5}}{2})^n = \frac{1}{2^n} \sum_{k} \binom{n}{k}(\sqrt{5})^k = \frac{1}{2^n} \sum_{k} \binom{n}{k} 5^{\frac{k}{2}} {}$

${} \hat{\phi}^n = (\frac{1-\sqrt{5}}{2})^n = \frac{1}{2^n} \sum_{k} \binom{n}{k}(-\sqrt{5})^k = \frac{1}{2^n} \sum_{k} \binom{n}{k} (-1)^k5^{\frac{k}{2}} {}$

${} F_n = \frac{1}{2^n\sqrt{5}}\sum_{k} \binom{n}{k}5^{\frac{k}{2}}(1-(-1)^k) = \frac{1}{2^{n-1}} \sum_{2 \not\mid k} \binom{n}{k}5^{\frac{k-1}{2}}{}$
