$F_0 = 0^2, F_1 = 1^2, F_{12} = 12^2$

Prove by induction that

$\forall n \leq 13: F_n > n^2$

$F_{13} = 233 > 13^2$

$F_{14} = 377 > 14^2$

$\forall n \geq 15: n - 1 \geq 13, n - 2 \geq 13$

$F_n = F_{n-1} + F_{n-2} > (n - 1)^2 + (n - 2)^2 = 2n^2-6n+5 = n^2 + (n-2)(n-3) > n^2$
