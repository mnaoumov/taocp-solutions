# 1.2.8-28

${} F_{n+1} - \phi F_n = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}(\phi^{n+1} - \hat{\phi}^{n+1}) - \frac{\phi}{\sqrt{5}}(\phi^{n} - \hat{\phi}^{n}) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}(\phi^{n+1} - \hat{\phi}^{n+1} - \phi^{n+1} + \phi \hat{\phi}^n) = \hat{\phi}^n \frac{\phi-\hat{\phi}}{\sqrt{5}} = \hat{\phi}^n  {}$
