$F_0 = 0, F_1 = 1, F_5 = 5$

Prove by induction that

$\forall n \leq 6: F_n > n$

$F_6 = 8 > 6$

$F_7 = 13 > 7$

$\forall n \geq 8: n - 1 \geq 6, n - 2 \geq 6$

$F_n = F_{n-1} + F_{n-2} > (n - 1) + (n - 2) = 2n-3 = n + (n-3) > n$
