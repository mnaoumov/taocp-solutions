$n$ is not prime, $n > 4$ $\implies n = kl, 2 \leq k \leq l, l \geq 3$

$F_l \geq 3 \implies F_l > 1$

$F_n = F_{kl}, F_l \mid F_{n}, F_n > F_l \implies \frac{F_n}{F_l} > 1 \implies F_n$ is not prime

For $n = 4, k=l=2, l \not \geq 3, F_n = 3$ - exception.
