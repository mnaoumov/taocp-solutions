# 1.2.1-4

${} F_1 = 1 \geq \phi^{1-1} {}$

${} F_2 = 1 \geq \phi^{2-1} {}$

${} \forall n \geq 3 {}$ Assume ${} \forall k, 1 \leq k < n: F_k \geq \phi^{k-1} {}$

${} F_{n} = F_{n-1} + F_{n-2} \geq \phi^{n-2} + \phi^{n-3} = \phi^{n-3}(\phi + 1) = \phi^{n-3} \cdot \phi^2 = \phi^{n-1} {}$

**Q.E.D.**
