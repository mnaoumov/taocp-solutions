# 1.2.4-47

${} p > 2 {}$ - prime.

${} p = 2 w + 1 {}$

${} b_k := \lfloor{\frac{2kq}{p}}\rfloor {}$

${} a_k := ((-1)^{b_k} 2kq) \mod p {}$

## a)

${} \lnot(p \backslash q) {}$

${} B := \{ 2, 4, \ldots, 2w \} {}$

${} A := \{ a_1, a_2, \ldots a_w \} {}$

Let ${} 2kq = sp + t, 0 \leq t \leq p - 1 {}$

${} a_k \equiv (-1)^s(sp+t) \equiv (-1)^s t \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ p) {}$

### 1

${} 2 \backslash s \implies s = 2u {}$

${} a_k \equiv (-1)^{2u} t = t \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ p) {}$

${} t = 2kq - 2up \equiv 0 \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ 2) {}$

${} t = 2v, 0 \leq v \leq \frac{p-1}{2} = w {}$

${} a_k = 2v \in B {}$

### 2

${} \lnot(2 \backslash s) \implies s = 2u + 1 {}$

${} a_k \equiv (-1)^{2u+1} t = -t \equiv p - t \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ p) {}$

${} t = 2kq - (2u+1)p \equiv -p \equiv 1 \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ 2) {}$

${} t = 2v + 1, 0 \leq v \leq \frac{p-3}{2} = w - 1 {}$

${} p - t = 2 (w - v)  {}$

${} a_k = 2(w-v) \in B {}$

---

${} \implies \forall k, 1 \leq k \leq w: a_k \in B \implies A \subset B {}$

Let ${} 1 \leq k < l \leq w {}$

Assume ${} a_k = a_l {}$

${} (-1)^{b_k} 2kq \equiv (-1)^{b_l} 2lq \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ p) {}$

${} -1 \equiv p - 1  \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ p) {}$

${} (p - 1) \perp p, 2 \perp p, q \perp p {}$

${} \implies ((-1)^{b_l} 2q) \perp p {}$

${} l \equiv (-1)^{b_k-b_l}k \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ p) {}$

### 1

If ${} 2 \backslash (b_k - b_l) {}$

${} l \equiv k \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ p \implies l = k{}$ - contradiction

### 2

If ${} \lnot(2 \backslash (b_k - b_l)) {}$

${} l \equiv -k \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ p \implies l = p - k \geq (2w+1)-w = w + 1 {}$ - contradiction

---

${} \implies a_k \neq a_l \implies \#A = w \implies A = B {}$

${} a_1a_2\cdots a_w \equiv (-1)^{b_1+b_2+\cdots + b_w} (2q)(4q) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (2wq) = (-1)^{\sigma} (2 \cdot 4 \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (2w))q^w \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ p) {}$

${} A = B \implies a_1a_2 \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot a_w = 2 \cdot 4 \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (2w) \perp p{}$

${} \implies (\frac{q}{p}) = q^w = (-1)^\sigma {}$
