# 1.2.4-20

${} ax \equiv by, a \equiv b\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ m), a \perp m {}$

[1.2.4-19](<./1.2.4-19.md>) ${} \implies \exists a': aa' \equiv 1\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ m) {}$

Law A ${} \implies ba' \equiv aa' \equiv 1\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ m) {}$

${} axa' \equiv bya'\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ m) {}$

${} x(aa') \equiv y(ba')\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ m) {}$

${} x(aa') \equiv x\cdot 1 \equiv x\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ m) {}$

${} y(na') \equiv y\cdot 1 \equiv y\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ m) {}$

${} \implies x \equiv y\ (\mathrm{modulo}\ m) \implies {}$ Law B.
