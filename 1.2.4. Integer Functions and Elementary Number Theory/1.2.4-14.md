# 1.2.4-14

${} x \mod 3 = 2, x \mod 5 = 3 {}$

${} x \mod 3 = 2 \implies x \mod 15 \in \{ 2, 5, 8, 11, 14 \} {}$

${} x \mod 5 = 3 \implies x \mod 15 \in \{ 3, 8, 13 \} {}$

${} \implies x \mod 15 = 8 {}$
