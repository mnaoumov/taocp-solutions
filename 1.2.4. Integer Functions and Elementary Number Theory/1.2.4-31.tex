Let \( r \perp s \)

\( g(rs) = \sum_{d \backslash (rs)} f(d) = \sum_{u\backslash r, v \backslash s, d = uv} f(d) = \sum_{d \backslash (rs)} f(d) = \sum_{u\backslash r, v \backslash s} f(uv) =: A \)

\( r \perp s \implies u \perp v \)

\( A = \sum_{d \backslash (rs)} f(d) = \sum_{u\backslash r, v \backslash s} f(u)f(v) = \sum_{u\backslash r} f(u) \cdot \sum_{v\backslash s} f(v) = g(r)g(s) \)
