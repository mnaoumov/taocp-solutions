# 1.2.4-39

### a)

${} f(x) = x - \frac{1}{2} {}$

${} \sum_{k=0}^{n-1} f(x+\frac{k}{n}) = \sum_{k=0}^{n-1} (x+\frac{k}{n}-\frac{1}{2}) = (x-\frac{1}{2})n + \frac{(n-1)n}{2n} = nx - \frac{1}{2} = f(nx) - \frac{1}{2} {}$
