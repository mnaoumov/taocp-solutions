$(-z)! \Gamma(z) = \frac{\pi}{\sin \pi z}\quad (16)$

$\pi z \prod_{n=1}^\infty (1- \frac{z^2}{n^2}) = \sin \pi z$

$\prod_{n=1}^\infty (1- \frac{z^2}{n^2}) = \prod_{n=1}^\infty \frac{(n-z)(n+z)}{n^2} = \prod_{n=1}^\infty \frac{(-z+n)(z+n)}{n \cdot n} = \frac{\Gamma(0+1)\Gamma(0+1)}{\Gamma(-z+1)\Gamma(z+1)} = \frac{1}{(-z)!z \Gamma(z)} = \frac{\sin \pi z}{\pi z} \implies (16)$
