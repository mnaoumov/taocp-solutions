\( u_k := D_x^k u \)

\( w_k := D_u^k w \)

\( D_x w = w_1 u_1 \)

We need to prove \( D_x^n w = \sum_{j=0}^n \sum_{\substack{k_1 + k_2 + \cdots + k_n = j\\ k_1 + 2k_2 + \cdots + nk_n = n\\k_1, k2_, \ldots, k_n \geq 0}} w_j \frac{n!}{k_1!(1!)^{k_1}\ldots k_n! (n!)^{k_n}} u_1^{k_1}\ldots u_n^{k_n} \)

Let \( \bar{k}=(k_1,k_2,\ldots): k_i \in \mathbb{N}_0, i \geq 0, \exists I \geq 1 \forall i \geq I: k_i = 0 \)

\( s(\bar{k}) := k_1 + k_2 + \cdots \)

\( t(\bar{k}) := 1k_1 + 2k_2 + \cdots \)

\( c(\bar{k}) := \frac{t(\bar{k})!}{k_1!(1!)^{k_1}\ldots k_n! (n!)^{k_n}\ldots} \)

\( u(\bar{k}) = u_1^{k_1}u_2^{k_2}\ldots \)

\( s(\bar{k} + \bar{l}) = s(\bar{k}) + s(\bar{l}) \)

\( t(\bar{k} + \bar{l}) = t(\bar{k}) + t(\bar{l}) \)

\( u(\bar{k} + \bar{l}) = u(\bar{k})u(\bar{l}) \)

With this notation, we need to prove

\( D_x^n w = \sum_{j=0}^n \sum_{\substack{\bar{k}\\ s(\bar{k}) = j\\ t(\bar{k}) = n}} w_j c(\bar{k})u(\bar{k}) = \sum_{\substack{\bar{k}\\ t(\bar{k}) = n}} w_{s(\bar{k})} c(\bar{k})u(\bar{k}) =: A_n(w) \)

Prove by induction

\( n = 1: D_x^n w = w_1u_1 \)

\( t(\bar{k}) = 1 \iff \bar{k} = (1,0,0,\ldots) = \bar{e}_1 \)

 \( A_1(w) = w_{s(\bar{e}_1)}c(\bar{e}_1))u(\bar{e}_1)) = w_1 \cdot 1 \cdot u_1 \)

\( (n \implies n + 1) \)

\( D_x^{n+1} w = D_x^1 (D_x^{n} w) = D_x^1(A_n(w)) = \sum_{\substack{\bar{k}\\ t(\bar{k}) = n}} c(\bar{k}) D_x^1(w_{s(\bar{k})} u(\bar{k})) = \sum_{\substack{\bar{k}\\ t(\bar{k}) = n}} c(\bar{k})(D_x^1w_{s(\bar{k})} \cdot u(\bar{k}) + w_{s(\bar{k})} \cdot D_x^1 u(\bar{k})) \)

\( D_x^1w_{s(\bar{k})} = D_u^1w_{s(\bar{k})} \cdot D_x^1 u = w_{s(\bar{k}) + 1} u_1 = w_{s(\bar{k} + \bar{e}_1)} u(\bar{e}_1) \)

\( D_x^1w_{s(\bar{k})} \cdot u(\bar{k}) = w_{s(\bar{k} + \bar{e}_1)} u(\bar{k} + \bar{e}_1) \)

\( \bar{e}_0 := (0,0,\ldots), k_0 := 1 \)

\( D_x^1w_{s(\bar{k})} \cdot u(\bar{k}) = k_0 w_{s(\bar{k} + \bar{e}_1 - \bar{e}_0)} u(\bar{k} + \bar{e}_1 - \bar{e}_0) \)

\( D_x^1 u(\bar{k})) = D_x^1 \left(\prod_{i \geq 1}u_i^{k_i}\right) = \sum_{r \geq 1} \left(\prod_{\substack{i \geq 1\\ i \neq r}}u_i^{k_i} \cdot D_x^1(u_r^{k_r})\right) = \sum_{r \geq 1} \left( u(\bar{k} - k_r \bar{e}_r) \cdot D_x^1(u_r^{k_r}) \right) \)

\( D_x^1(u_r^{k_r}) = k_r u_r^{k_r-1} D_x^1(u_r) = k_r u_r^{k_r - 1} u_{r+1} = k_r u\bigl((k_r-1)\bar{e}_r + \bar{e}_{r+1}\bigr) \)

\( D_x^1 u(\bar{k})) = \sum_{r \geq 1} k_r \left( u\bigl(\bar{k} - k_r \bar{e}_r\bigr) \cdot u\bigl((k_r-1)\bar{e}_r + \bar{e}_{r+1}\bigr) \right) = \sum_{r \geq 1} k_r u(\bar{k} - \bar{e}_r + \bar{e}_{r+1}) \)

\( s(\bar{k} - \bar{e}_r + \bar{e}_{r+1}) = s(\bar{k}) - s(\bar{e}_r) + s(\bar{e}_{r+1}) = s(\bar{k}) \)

\( D_x^{n+1}w = \sum_{\substack{\bar{k}\\ t(\bar{k}) = n}} c(\bar{k}) \sum_{r\geq 0} k_r w_{s(\bar{k} - \bar{e}_r + \bar{e}_{r+1})} u(\bar{k} - \bar{e}_r + \bar{e}_{r+1}) \)

\( t(\bar{k} - \bar{e}_r + \bar{e}_{r+1}) = t(\bar{k}) - t(\bar{e}_r) + t(\bar{e}_{r+1}) = n - r + (r+1) = n + 1 \)

\( D_x^{n+1} w = \sum_{\substack{\bar{l}\\ t(\bar{l}) = n + 1 \\ }} w_{s(\bar{l})} u(\bar{l}) \sum_{r \geq 0} \sum_{\substack{\bar{k}\\ t(\bar{k}) = n \\ \bar{l} = \bar{k} - \bar{e}_r + \bar{e}_{r+1}}} k_r c(\bar{k}) \)

We need to prove \( c(\bar{l}) = \sum_{r \geq 0} \sum_{\substack{\bar{k}\\ t(\bar{k}) = n \\ \bar{l} = \bar{k} - \bar{e}_r + \bar{e}_{r+1}}} k_r c(\bar{k}) =: d(\bar{l}) \)

If \( l_{r+1} = 0 \implies k_{r+1} = -1 < 0 \implies \) such \( \bar{k} \) does not exist.

If \( l_{r+1} > 0 \implies \bar{k} = \bar{l} + \bar{e}_{r} - \bar{e}_{r + 1} \implies k_r = l_r + 1, r \geq 1 \)

\( d(\bar{l}) = k_0 c(\bar{l} + \bar{e}_0 - \bar{e}_1)[l_1 > 0] + \sum_{\substack{r \geq 1\\ l_{r+1} > 0}} (l_r + 1) c(\bar{l} + \bar{e}_{r} - \bar{e}_{r + 1}) = \frac{n!}{\prod_{j \geq 2} l_j!(j!)^{l_j} \cdot (l_1-1)!}[l_1 > 0] + \sum_{\substack{r \geq 1\\ l_{r+1} > 0}} (l_r + 1) \frac{n!}{\prod_{\substack{j \geq 1 \\ j \neq r, r+1}} l_j!(j!)^{l_j} \cdot (l_r+1)!(r!)^{l_r+1} (l_{r+1}-1)!((r+1)!)^{l_{r+1}-1}} = \)

\( = \frac{c(\bar{l})}{n+1} (l_1 [l_1 > 0] + \sum_{\substack{r \geq 1 \\ l_{r+1} > 0}} (l_r + 1)) \frac{l_{r+1} (r+1)!}{(l_r + 1)r!} = \frac{c(\bar{l})}{n+1}(l_1 + \sum_{r \geq 1} l_{r+1}(r+1)) = \frac{c(\bar{l})}{n+1} t(\bar{l}) = c(\bar{l}) \)
