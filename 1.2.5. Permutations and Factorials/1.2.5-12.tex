\( = \sum_{i \geq 0 }^r a_i p^{i} \sum_{k=1}^{i}{p^{-k}} = \sum_{i \geq 0}{a_ip^i \frac{1 - p^{-i}}{p-1}} = \frac{1}{p-1} \sum_{i\geq 0}(a_ip^i-a_i) = \frac{n - \sum_{i\geq 0}a_i}{p-1} \)
