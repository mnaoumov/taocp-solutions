\( p_{n,n-1} = n(n-1)(n-2) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (n-(n-1)+1) = n(n-1) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot 2 = \frac{n(n-1) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot 2 \cdot 1}{1} = p_{nn} = n! \)

Choosing \( n - 1 \) objects out of \( n \) and arranging them in a row, leaves one object left, and there is a single way to add it to the end of the previous row. That's why \( p_{n,n-1} = p_{nn} \)
