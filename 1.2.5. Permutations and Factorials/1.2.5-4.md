# 1.2.5-4

${} \log_{10}{1000!} = 2567.60464\ldots {}$

${} 1000! = 10^{2567.60464\ldots} = 10^{2567} \cdot 10^{0.60464\ldots} {}$

${} \log_{10}{4} = 0.6020\ldots {}$

${} \log_{10}{5} = 0.6989\ldots {}$

${} \log_{10}{4} < 0.60464\ldots < \log_{10}{5} {}$

${} 4 < 10^{0.60464\ldots} < 5 {}$

${} 10^{2567} \cdot 4 < 1000! < 10^{2567} \cdot 5 {}$

${} \implies 1000! {}$ has ${} 2568 {}$ digits, most significant is ${} 4 {}$, least significant is ${} 0 {}$
