Introduce the following notation: \( a = \langle{b} \rangle_p \overset{\mathrm{def}}{\iff} a \mod p = b \mod p \)

\( n = \sum_{i \geq 0}{a_i p^i} \)

\( a_i = 0, i > k \)

\( n_j := \lfloor{\frac{n}{p^j}}\rfloor = \sum_{i \geq j}{a_i p^{i-j}} \)

\( n = n_0 = pn_1 + a_0 \)

\( n_k = a_k \)

\( n! = \)

\( = \overbrace{1 \cdot 2  \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (p - 1)}^A \cdot \overbrace{p}^B \cdot \)

\( \cdot \overbrace{(p+1)(p+2) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (p+(p-1))}^A \cdot \overbrace{2p}^B \cdot \)

\( \cdot \overbrace{(2p+1)(2p+2) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (2p+(p-1))}^A \cdot \overbrace{3p}^B \cdot \)

\( \cdots \)

\( \cdot \overbrace{((n_1-1)p+1)((n_1-1)p+2) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot ((n_1-1)p+(p-1))}^A \cdot \overbrace{n_1p}^B \cdot \)

\( \cdot \overbrace{(n_1p+1)(n_1p+2) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (n_1p+a_0)}^C \)

\( \forall j, 0 \leq j \leq n_1 - 1: (jp+1)(jp+2) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot (jp+p-1) = \langle{1 \cdot 2 \cdots (p-1)}\rangle_p = \langle{(p-1)!}\rangle_p = \langle{-1}\rangle_p \)

\( \implies A = \langle(-1)^{n_1}\rangle_p \)

\( B = n_1!p^{n_1} \)

\( C = \langle{a_0!}\rangle_p \)

\( n! = n_0! = ABC = \langle{(-1)^{n_1}}\rangle_p n_1!p^{n_1}\langle{a_0!}\rangle_p \)

Similarly

\( n_1! = \langle{(-1)^{n_2}}\rangle_p n_2!p^{n_2}\langle{a_1!}\rangle_p \)

\( \vdots \)

\( n_{k-1}! = \langle{(-1)^{n_k}}\rangle_p n_k!p^{n_k}\langle{a_{k-1}!}\rangle_p \)

\( n_k! = a_k! \)

\( n! = (\langle{(-1)^{n_1}}\rangle_p p^{n_1}\langle{a_0!}\rangle_p) \cdot (\langle{(-1)^{n_2}}\rangle_p p^{n_2}\langle{a_1!}\rangle_p) \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot \cdot (\langle{(-1)^{n_k}}\rangle_p p^{n_k}\langle{a_{k-1}!}\rangle_p) \cdot a_k! = \)

\( = \langle{(-1)^{n_1+n_2+ \cdots + n_k}}\rangle_p p^{n_1+n_2+\cdots +n_k}\langle{a_0!a_1! \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot a_k!}\rangle_p \)

\( n_1+n_2+\cdots+n_k = \mu(n,p) \)

\( \frac{n!}{p^{\mu(n,p)}} = \langle{(-1)^{\mu(n,p)}a_0!a_1! \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot a_k!}\rangle_p \)

\( \frac{n!}{p^{\mu(n,p)}} \equiv (-1)^{\mu(n,p)}a_0!a_1! \cdot\ \cdots\ \cdot a_k! \ (\mathrm{modulo}\ p) \)
