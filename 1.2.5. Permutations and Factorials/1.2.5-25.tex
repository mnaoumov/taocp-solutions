\( x^{\underline{m+n}} = x(x-1)(x-2)\cdots(x-m+1)\cdot(x-m)(x-m-1)\cdots(x-m-n+1) = x^{\underline{m}}(x-m)^{\underline{n}} \)

\( x^{\overline{m+n}} = (-1)^{m+n}(-x)^{\underline{m+n}} = (-1)^{m+n}(-x)^{\underline{m}}(-x-m)^{\underline{n}} = x^{\overline{m}} (x+m)^{\overline{n}} \)

For non-integer

\( x^{\underline{m+n}} = \frac{x!}{(x-m-n)!} = \frac{x!}{(x-m)!} \cdot \frac{(x-m!)}{(x-m-n)!} = x^{\underline{m}}(x-m)^{\underline{n}} \)

\( x^{\overline{m+n}} = \frac{\Gamma(x + m + n)}{\Gamma(x)} = \frac{\Gamma(x+m)}{\Gamma(x)} \cdot \frac{\Gamma(x+m+n)}{\Gamma(x+m)} = x^{\overline{m}} (x+m)^{\overline{n}} \)
