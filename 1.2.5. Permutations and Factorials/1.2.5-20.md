# 1.2.5-20

${} |\Gamma_m(x) - \int_0^m e^{-t}t^{x-1}dt| \leq \int_0^m t^{x-1}|(1-\frac{t}{m})^m - e^{-t}|dt \leq \frac{1}{m}\int_0^m t^{x-1}t^2e^{-t}dt \leq {}$

${} \leq \frac{1}{m} \int_0^{\infty} t^{x+1}e^{-t}dt \to 0, m \to \infty {}$

${} \implies \Gamma(x) = \int_0^{\infty} e^{-t}t^{x-1}dt {}$
