# 1.2.5-9

${} (\frac{1}{2})! = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2} {}$

${} (\frac{1}{2})! = \frac{1}{2} \Gamma(\frac{1}{2}) \implies \Gamma(\frac{1}{2}) = \sqrt{\pi} {}$

${} \Gamma(\frac{1}{2}) = (-\frac{1}{2})\Gamma(-\frac{1}{2}) \implies \Gamma(-\frac{1}{2}) = -2\sqrt{\pi} {}$
