# 1.2.5-6

${} \mu(n,p) = \sum_{k > 0}{\lfloor{\frac{n}{p^k}}\rfloor} {}$

${} \mu(20,2) = 10 + 5 + 2 + 1 = 18 {}$

${} \mu(20,3) = 6 + 2 = 8 {}$

${} \mu(20,5) = 4 {}$

${} \mu(20,7) = 2 {}$

${} \mu(20,11) = 1 {}$

${} \mu(20,13) = 1 {}$

${} \mu(20,17) = 1 {}$

${} \mu(20,19) = 1 {}$
