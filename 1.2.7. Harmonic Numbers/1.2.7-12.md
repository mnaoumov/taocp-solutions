# 1.2.7-12

${} H_{\infty}^{(1000)} {}$

[1.2.7-3](<./1.2.7-3.md>) ${} \implies H_{\infty}^{(10001)} \leq \frac{2^{1000-1}}{2^{1000-1}-1} = 1 + \frac{1}{2^{1000-1}-1} {}$

${} 2^{1000-1}-1 = \frac{1}{2} \cdot (2^{10})^{100} - 1 \geq \frac{1}{2} 10^{300} - 1 > 10^{100} \implies \frac{1}{2^{1000-1}-1} < 10^{-100} \implies H_{\infty}^{(10001)} \approx 1 {}$
