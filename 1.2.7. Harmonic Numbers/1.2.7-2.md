# 1.2.7-2

${} H_1 = 1 {}$

${} H_{2^{k+1}} - H_{2^{k}} = \sum_{j=2^{k}+1}^{2^{k+1}} \frac{1}{j} \leq \sum_{j=2^{k}+1}^{2^{k+1}} \frac{1}{2^{k}} = 2^{k} \cdot \frac{1}{2^{k}} = 1 {}$

${} H_{2^m} - H_1 = \sum_{k=0}^{m-1} (H_{2^{k+1}}-H_{2^{k}}) \leq \sum_{k=0}^{m-1} 1 = m {}$

${} H_{2^m} \leq 1 + m {}$
