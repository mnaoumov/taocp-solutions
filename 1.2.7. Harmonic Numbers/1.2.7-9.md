# 1.2.7-9

${} F(x) = \sum_{k=1}^n \binom{n}{k} x^kH_k  {}$

${} F(-1) = \sum_{k=1}^n \binom{n}{k} (-1)^k H_k = \sum_{k=1}^n \binom{n}{k} (-1)^k \sum_{j=1}^k \frac{1}{j} = \sum_{j=1}^n \frac{1}{j} \sum_{k=j}^n \binom{n}{k} (-1)^k {}$

${} \sum_{k=j}^n \binom{n}{k} (-1)^k = \sum_{k=1}^n \binom{n}{k} (-1)^k - \sum_{k=0}^{j-1} \binom{n}{k} (-1)^k = 0 - \sum_{k=0}^{j-1} \binom{n}{k} (-1)^k \overset{1.2.6(18)}{=} {}$

${} = -(-1)^{j-1} \binom{n-1}{j-1} = (-1)^{j} \binom{n-1}{j-1} {}$

${} F(-1) = \sum_{j=1}^n \frac{1}{j} (-1)^{j} \binom{n-1}{j-1} = \sum_{j=1}^n \frac{1}{n} (-1)^{j} \binom{n}{j} = \frac{1}{n} (\sum_{j=0}^n (-1)^{j} \binom{n}{j} - (-1)^0\binom{n}{0}) = -\frac{1}{n} {}$
