\( y := x - 1 \)

\( \sum_{k=1}^n \frac{x^k}{k} = \sum_{m=1}^n \frac{x^m}{m} = \sum_{m=1}^n \frac{(1+y)^m}{m} = \sum_{m=1}^n \frac{1}{m} \sum_{k=0}^m \binom{m}{k} y^k = \sum_{k=0}^m y^k \sum_{\substack{1 \leq m \leq n\\m \geq k}} \frac{1}{m} \binom{m}{k} = \)

\( = y^0 \sum_{m=1}^n \frac{1}{m}\binom{m}{0} + \sum_{k=1}^m y^k \sum_{m=k}^n \frac{1}{m} \binom{m}{k} =: A \)

\( y^0 \sum_{m=1}^n \frac{1}{m}\binom{m}{0} = \sum_{m=1}^n \frac{1}{m} = H_n \)

\( \sum_{m=k}^n \frac{1}{m} \binom{m}{k} = \frac{1}{k} \sum_{m=k}^n \binom{m-1}{k-1} = \frac{1}{k} \sum_{m=k-1}^{n-1} \binom{m}{k-1} = \frac{1}{k} \sum_{m=0}^{n-1} \binom{m}{k-1} = \frac{1}{k} \binom{n}{k} \)

\( A = H_n + \sum_{k=1}^m \binom{n}{k}\frac{y^k}{k} \)
