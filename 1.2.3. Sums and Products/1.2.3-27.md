# 1.2.3-27

Induction by ${} n {}$

${} n = 1 {}$

${} \prod_{j=1}^1 (1-a_j) = 1-a_1 \geq 1 - \sum_{j=1}^1 a_j {}$

${} (n \implies n+1) {}$

${} \prod_{j=1}^{n+1} (1-a_j) = (1-a_{n+1}) \cdot \prod_{j=1}^{n} (1-a_j) \geq (1-a_{n+1}) \cdot (1 - \sum_{j=1}^n a_j) = 1 - \sum_{j=1}^n a_j - a_{n+1} + \sum_{j=1}^n a_ja_{n+1} \geq 1 - \sum_{j=1}^{n+1} a_j {}$
