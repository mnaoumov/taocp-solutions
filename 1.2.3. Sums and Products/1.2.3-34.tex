\( S = \sum_{k=1}^n \frac{\prod_{1 \leq r \leq n, r \neq m} (x + k - r)}{\prod_{1 \leq r \leq n, r \neq k} (k - r)} \)

\( x_k := k \)

\( f(t) := \prod_{1 \leq r \leq n, r \neq m} (x + t - r) = \sum_{i=0}^{n-1} a_it^i \)

\( p(t) = \prod_{1 \leq r \leq n} (t-x_r) \)

\( \prod_{1 \leq r \leq n, r \neq k} (x_k - x_r) = p'(x_k) \)

\( S = \sum_{k=1}^n \frac{f(x_k)}{p'(x_k)} = \sum_{i=0}^{n-1} a_i \sum_{k=1}^n \frac{x_k^i}{p'(x_k)} \)

By [1.2.3-33](<./1.2.3-33.md>):

\( S = a_0 \cdot 0 + a_1 \cdot 0 + \cdots + a_{n-2} \cdot 0 + a_{n-1} \cdot 1 = a_{n-1} \)

\( f(t) = t^{n-1} + g(t), \deg d \leq n - 1 \implies a_{n-1} = 1 \)

\( S = 1 \)
