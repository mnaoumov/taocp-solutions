# 1.2.2-19

${} \log_{10}{2} = 0.30102999\ldots {}$

${} \log_{10}{2^{47}} = 47 \log_{10}{2} = 47 \cdot (0.30102999\ldots) = 14.14840963\ldots {}$

${} 2^{47} > 10^{14} \implies {}$ 47 bits is enough to represent ${} n {}$ decimal digits.
