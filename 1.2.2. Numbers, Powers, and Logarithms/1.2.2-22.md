# 1.2.2-22

${} \ln{x} + \log_{10}{x} = \frac{\lg{x}}{\lg{e}} +  \frac{\lg{x}}{\lg{10}} = \lg{x} \cdot (\ln{2} + \log_{10}{2}) = \lg{x} \cdot  (0.693147180559945\ldots + 0.301029995663981\ldots) = \lg{x} \cdot (0.994177176223926\ldots) \approx \lg{x} {}$
