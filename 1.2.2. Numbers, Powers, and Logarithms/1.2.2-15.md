# 1.2.2-15

${} \log_b{\frac{x}{y}} \overset{?}{=} \log_b{x} - \log_b{y}\ (1) {}$

${} b^{\log_b{\frac{x}{y}}} = \frac{x}{y} = \frac{b^{\log_b{x}}}{b^{\log_b{y}}} = b^{\log_b{x} - \log_b{y}} \implies (1) {}$
