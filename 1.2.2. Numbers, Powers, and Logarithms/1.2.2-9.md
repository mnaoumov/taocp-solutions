# 1.2.2-9

Let's ${} x = \frac{m}{n}, y = \frac{p}{q} {}$

${} b^{x+y}=b^{\frac{m}{n} + \frac{p}{q}} = b^{\frac{mq+np}{nq}} = \sqrt[nq]{b^{mq+np}} = \sqrt[nq]{b^{mq} \cdot b^{np}} = \sqrt[nq]{b^{mq}} \cdot \sqrt[nq]{b^{np}} = b^{\frac{mq}{nq}} \cdot b^{\frac{np}{nq}} = b^{\frac{m}{n}} \cdot b^{\frac{p}{q}} = b^x \cdot b^y {}$

${} b^{xy} = b^{\frac{m}{n}\cdot\frac{p}{q}} = b^{\frac{mp}{nq}} = \sqrt[nq]{b^{mp}} = \sqrt[nq]{(b^{m})^p} = \sqrt[nq]{((\sqrt[n]{b^m})^n)^{p}} = \sqrt[nq]{(\sqrt[n]{b^m})^{np}} = (b^{\frac{m}{n}})^{\frac{np}{nq}} (b^{\frac{m}{n}})^{\frac{p}{q}} = (b^x)^y {}$
